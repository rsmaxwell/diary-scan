A fine morning. Been a slight frost with a little snow during the night. Wind NW. Sea much fallen, so that I think many ships will get to sea.

Received a letter from WBS with copies of letters written to the Duke & Lord Beverley applying for a commission for me in the Northumbria Militia. Answered his letter.

Wrote to Bevor's brother to come down to Shields to make some arrangements with regard to the fasern after seeing J Tinley upon the subject.

Dined at home. Spent the evening & suppered with H Harry.
