A very rainy & foggy day & blowing strong from SE & high sea. Sent the bill for the Hope's cargo by Dunn the coachman to Mr Heindhaigh. Received a letter from Margaret Scott of Crathorn, I having forgot to send her 20/- her quarters allowance. Sent it by return of post. Dined at home. At the billiard room in the afternoon & the whist club in the evening. Drew upon Chapman for £5
