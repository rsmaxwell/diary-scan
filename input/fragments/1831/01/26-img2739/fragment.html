A continuance of sharp frost. Wind NNW. Very little additional snow fallen in the night. Went by Dunn's coach to Newcastle, there being a meeting of the leiabenantcy & saw Dixon who promised to speak to Captain Carlson respecting a commission for me in the military. Had some dinner with Emma Galbraith & returned by Duna in the afternoon.

Eat a couple of mutton chops at 6 o'clock & finished in the evening at the Albion
