A fine wintery morning having been a very heavy fall of snow during the night. Wind WNW.

Received a letter from B B Galbraith. A bill for £6.0.10 drawn by Stodter of the Eligible club presented for payment, but I did not take it up having no notice. Dined at 5 o'clock with J Dale & in the evening attended the ball at the George which was the best we have had for a long time. Got home at ¼ past 3 o'clock
