Most unpleasant weather, damp & uncomfortable. Wind SW. Received a letter yesterday from Stephenson of the ship being out on Thursday delivering 282 char & expecting to get his ballast on Saturday. Received the Hope's afsales with a balance note for £93.7. which I paid into Chapmans & drew for £50 with which I paid the bells for the Archangel voyage viz: swan £___ sharp £___ & spares £___. At the theatre in the evening, Michaels benefit. A good house.
