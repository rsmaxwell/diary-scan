A damp morning, blowing fresh from NNE. Went by steam to Newcastle. Fixed the Hope to load ords Redheught to be upheld 11/- freight go to WBS as factor, & all profit above 11/- to be divided. Returned by coach. Attended the annual meeting of the Eligible and United clubs to alter & receive the rules etc. Sat down a large party to dinner afterwards. At the Mulstrum.
