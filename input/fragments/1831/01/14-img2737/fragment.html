A dull foggy morning but rather frosty. Wind West. Got the buf for the ship of Gibson. This voyage 6 ____ @ 42/- went on board with the buf. Gave Geo Stoss £2 on account. Five more keels came down this morning. At the billiard room in the afternoon & at the theatre in the evening. The last time of the company performing this season. Engaged to dine with J Telbs but did not go, rather unwell.
