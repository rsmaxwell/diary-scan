A fine morning after a very heavy fall of rain during the night. Wind SSW. Afterwards changeable between N & S by E. After breakfast went to Codlings, Stephenson met me there & we got all my luggage on board the Crisis & about 12 o'clock having got all her ballast in, she weighed anchor & dropped down to Greenwich where we were enabled to sail down the reach & we got to Woolwich between 2 & 3 o'clock. The tide here low we took the ground & there remained until 10 o'clock at which time I had gone to bed but the ship got under way & beat down to earth & brought up between 1 & 2 o'clock in the morning
