A continuance of fine weather. Wind during the day varying between SE & SE. At church in the morning. Saw Mr Spencer yesterday who agreed to renew our bill upon our rubbing off £100 or £150. Wrote to WBS. Dined at home. Walked to Tynemouth in the afternoon. Called at Cunningham's Dr Drury there & had some excellent glenlivet with them. Called afterwards at the castle, sat with the Marines & at night again with Bess
