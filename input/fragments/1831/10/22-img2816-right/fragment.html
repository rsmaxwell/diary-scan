A dull cloudy morning blowing hard from the SW.

Redley & I walked to the newsroom, thence to Preston where we parted, he going on to Hartley. Saw Moore & urged him to thrash & sell everything. Dined at 5 o'clock alone. Stephenson called & informed me the Eligible Committee had been on board surveyed the stores etc. & reported the ship complete. Had got the dung out, the ship upon the shore to look at & clean her bottom. Found her in good order, only a butts and or two wanting caulking.

With Bess in the evening
