Turned the hands up about 5 o'clock being low water & the wind at SSW I got up before 6 o'clock. The people busy getting the anchor & setting sails. Had a fine run during the day down swin, studding sails set but not much wind which varied between SE & S by W. At ½ past 8 o'clock at night off Aldeburgh & beautiful clear weather but a strong flood tide against us. Turned in about 9 o'clock
