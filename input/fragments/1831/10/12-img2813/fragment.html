Fine weather. Wind WSW. Went up in a gig to Newcastle. Called & saw Emma Galbraith who was looking well. Payed Mrs Noakes for Emma G's board and education up to June £39.10/-. Afterwards walked out to Benton where I dined & stayed all night. Dixon Clark there, he slept in Newcastle to take the coach in the morning for Oxford. Tom Smith left them in the morning for Togston
