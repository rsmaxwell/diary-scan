A dull morning. Heavy SW wind. Called at Chapman's & settled for WBS to take up the £300 note in London. They to give the executors account credit for £200 & take our note at 6 months for it. Wrote to WBS in answer to his letter received yesterday about it. Dined at home at 2 o'clock & Lockwood & I walked up afterwards to Billy Mill & took wine with Charlton.

At Bess in the evening

Got £5 of GH
