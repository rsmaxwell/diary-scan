At anchor off Erith. A cloudy morning with showers. Wind fresh from SSW after washing the decks & trimming ballast, the ship having been by the stern, we got under way at about 11 o'clock & beat down to the home light where we brought up at 6 o'clock. The wind having changed during the day to S by W: S: & S by E blowing fresh. Turned after a glass of grog between 8 & 9 o'clock
