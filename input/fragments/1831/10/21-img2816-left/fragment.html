A very fine morning. The air rather frosty. Wind NSW. Left Alnwick by the Wonder coach at 10 o'clock, got to Newcastle before 3 o'clock, took a Shields gig & got home in time to order dinner at 6 o'clock when Charles Redley came & stayed all night. I spoke to Colonel Colson yesterday respecting the second gazetteer stating that I still considered myself very unhandsomely treated. I showed him the Dukes letter but could get nothing out of him
