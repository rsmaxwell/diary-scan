A letter from WBS

A mild morning. Wind SW. A party of us went to Cullercoat Sands & found ourselves at Rifle & Pistol shooting at a target. Lunched at the castle & at 4 o'clock dined with J Redhead & Lockwood at the lodgings. I sent them a pair of soles. At night with Bess

This days papers brought an account of the Reform Bill being thrown out of the Lords by a majority of 41. The debate did not conclude till ½ past 6 in the morning. Much excitement in London.

A rainy afternoon & night
