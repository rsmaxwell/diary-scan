Beautiful mild weather. Wind in the morning SW. Changed in the afternoon to SSE. Left Benton directly after breakfast & walked out to Newcastle to meet Stephenson who arrived yesterday, fixed the hope to load Bell & Dixons Walls, but won't get coals for upwards of a fortnight. Took the steamer to Shields.

Found a letter from WBS with the Hopes freight account but no note, brokers commission being changed which WBS thought an error. Paid an average Eligible Club £14.9.3 & gave Stephenson £20 to pay wages. Dined at home at 5 o'clock. At the whist club in the evening & with Bess at night. A rainy & blowing night
