A very fair morning. Wind from SW. Got up early & took Dunn's gig to Newcastle. Took a place in the Alnwick coach at 10 o'clock. Got there before 3 o'clock where most of the officers who intended dining at the mess had arrived. Sat down to dinner at 6 o'clock. Spent a very pleasant & jovial evening & kept it up until 4 o'clock in the morning.
