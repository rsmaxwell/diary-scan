A cloudy morning with the appearance of showers having been showers during the night. Wind SE. Ben Galbraith & Robert set off in Dunn's gig to catch the Alnwick coach at Newcastle for Felton where Tom Smith's phaeton will meet them. I could not go having promised to continue my assistance to Mr Ilderton all day & very successful. Sat down to dinner at 5 o'clock. Called at the Albion in the evening.
