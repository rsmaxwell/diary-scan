Still delightful weather. After breakfast Tom & I set off for Newcastle in his jones Auisn. Stopped at Morpeth for two hours. Saw Shapton & got to Newcastle at 5 o'clock to dinner. Saw the High Sherriff, Mr Silvertop go out accompanied by a very numerous train of friends & carriages to meet the judges. The High Sheriff turned out very handsome. Took a gig in the evening & got home a little before 10 o'clock
