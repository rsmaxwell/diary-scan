A fine morning but very hot weather. Gave a draft on Cols for balance of Premium and Hope clubs £4.7.1 also a draft for £6.15 momo to be paid tomorrow. Attended Mr Ilderton's Committee room and came after Lenskill Street. Dined at home. At the Committee Room again in the afternoon. A fine shower of rain in the afterwards. At the theatre & Mulstrum in the evening
