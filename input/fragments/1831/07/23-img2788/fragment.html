A most beautiful day. Ben Galbraith, Rob Snowdon & I after breakfast, set off to fish at the Hermitage. I however turned off to begin at the Granary but the tide had risen too high. Walked up to the mill streams & fished there all the morning. Fanny & Bell Smith & Emma Galbraith came down & joined us & we all dined together at the Hermitage Inn. Fished up the waterside & Morick streams in the evening but had very poor sport. Got home to supper after spending a most delightful day.
