A dull cloudy morning & been some rain. Wind ENE. A very heavy rain for an hour or so which prevented us beginning our canvas until near 11 o'clock commencing at the Wooden bridge & canvassed all the upper part of the town. Milburn Place etc and were very successful. Lunched at Mr Barkers & canvassed Tynemouth in the evening. Dined at 8 o'clock & spent a jolly evening.
