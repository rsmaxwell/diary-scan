A most beautiful morning. Wind with the heat got to ENE. Arrived at Shields at 7 o'clock. Crossed the water & got home in time to go to the Low Lights & bath before breakfast. Dined at home. Being excessively sleepy, turned into bed for three hours. Walked after tea up to Preston through the fields to examine them & see moors.
