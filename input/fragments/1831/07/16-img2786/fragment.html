A fine morning. Breakfasted early & took the 8 o'clock steamer for Newcastle & at 1 o'clock got upon the coach for Felton where I arrived at a little after 1 o'clock. Had an early dinner & afterwards borrowed a nod of Melrose & had a few hours fishing but little success. Walked down in the cool of the evening to Togston. Got there at ½ past 6 o'clock. Found the Clutterbucks dining there.
