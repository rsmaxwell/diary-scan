Storm abated & finer weather. Wind SE and a rapid thaw.

Many ships on shore. Two on the Herd Sand & one a wreck, two on the rocks and broken to pieces, three under the castle light house too scattered to pieces mesher & boy of one drowned a smack at Cullercoats, all hands drowned. Three or four near Whitley, two broke to pieces immediately on coming on shore at night & crews drowned. Walked round by the rock & Tynemouth. Dreadful destruction.

Dined at home. At the billiard room all the afternoon & in the evening Mr Lockword came home with me & drank a glass of punch. Paid an average bill Eligible Club by draft for £6.0.10. Came on to blow almost as hard as ever. Many ships got in today.
