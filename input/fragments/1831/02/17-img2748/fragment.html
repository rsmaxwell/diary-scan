A fine morning blowing fresh at WNW. Went by the coach to Newcastle. Called at the club house to see Colonel Coulson who had left town in the morning. At D Dixons, saw Dixon & the ladies. Called on young Bigger who gave me the names of the trader men in London who have the regulation patterns. Walked down from Newcastle. Met Dr Drury & Mr Harrison near Mr Robson who were on their way to dine with Saddler at the Point & prevailed on me to accompany them. At the whist club & Mulstrum in the evening.
