A calm morning. Very foggy. Wind S by W & a frost.

Attended a meeting of the Eligible Club & the members agreed to resist Mr Kings of South Shields claim as being unjust.

Dined at home. At the billiard room in the afternoon & whist club in the evening.

Received a letter from WBS of the safe arrival of the Hope & a copy of a letter received from the Duke of Northumbria in answer to his application for a commission in the military for me; very polite.
