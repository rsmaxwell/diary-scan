A very stormy morning. Blew a gale of wind all night from ENE with heavy sleet & rain.

Two ships have got upon the Herd Sand. One from America, the other from ____

Settled with Robert & paid into Chapmans on executors account £65 of rents & drew for £18.11.6 to pay Mrs Noakes, late Miss Robinson for Emma Gilbraith boards etc.

Another small light vessel got upon the Herd.

At the news & billiard rooms all the morning. No moving out such stormy weather. Dined at 5 o'clock with J Tinley, a party. Attended an evening party at the Messes Frenches'
