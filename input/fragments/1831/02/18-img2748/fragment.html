A continuance of fine weather. Wind WNW.

Received a letter from WBS & after breakfast went on board the Jane at his request to see Mr Brown to desire him not to clear the Harmony or of cleared to order the master to stop below Gravesend until after the 1 March when the coal duties are to be taken off.

Attended a meeting at the Commercial & entered into resolutions to be followed up by petitions against the proposed carecase of duty upon north American timber.

Dined at home. At the billiard room in the afternoon. Suppered with H harry
