A most beautiful morning. Wind changed to WNW. Settled Robert's book & paid him £13.-1d my private expenses for the year up to this time. Looked into the Duke's court. Went to Tynemouth races. A great many people there. Dined at 4 o'clock at the Northumberland Alms with McBain & the jury. Called in the evening at the Mulstrum. Wind changed to east.
