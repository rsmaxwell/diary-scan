A beautiful morning, but cool wind East. Took the 12 o'clock steamer & got home to dinner at 2 o'clock. Walked to Tynemouth in the afternoon & Cullercoat Sands. All immense numbers of people there. H Harry, Cunningham, Jebbs & I took wine together at the Star & Garter. Returned to the Sands in the evening & had some fun there. Got home at 10 o'clock.
