A fine but rather damp morning. Wind ESE. The charter for the Hope arrived by the post from Hull. The Merchants Messrs Wade Sons & Forster Wybury & Hull. 70/ for deals & buttons. The latter not to exceed 6/8. Ordered 3 casks or pork of Bradshaws & 5 loaves of bread of Walkerley & a new main topgallantsail. Dined at home & in the afternoon H Harry gave the Doctor & me some wine at the Northumberland Alms. Afterwards at the Abbey & Mulstrum
