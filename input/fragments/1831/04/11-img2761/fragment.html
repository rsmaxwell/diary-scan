A beautiful spring morning. Been showers in the night. Wind WNW. Received a letter from WBS. Took a walk to Cullercoats with J Cunningham by the Sands. Had a party to dinner at 2 o'clock. H harry, Jebbs, Cunningham & C Ridley from Hartley spent a very pleasant day. E Charlton took wine with us. At the Mulstrum at night or rather in the morning
