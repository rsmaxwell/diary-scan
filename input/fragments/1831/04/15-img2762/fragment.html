A fine morning. Wind NW. Received the Hopes accounts & balance note £97.9/ = { balance accounts 47.9 : on account 50 } Received also a letter from Leadbottom, the introduction Newcastle w entry a statement of the account between us & Mrs Scott executors which I sent him.

Dined at home. On board the Harvy in the afternoon & went over the bar with: bound to Quebec. Finished off at the Albion & called at the Mulstrum
