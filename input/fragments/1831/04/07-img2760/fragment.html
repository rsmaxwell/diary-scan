A dull morning. Wind SW. Afterwards turned out a most beautiful day, wind a little to E of S. Wrote to WBS with a account of rents. Afterwards J Cunningham & I started & had a most delightful walk to Hastley where we dined with C Ridley & walked home again in the evening. But before we reached Tynemouth at 6 o'clock we were caught in a heavy rain & got thoroughly wet. After taking a glass of whiskey Toddy with JC to keep the cold out, made the best of my way home & changed my cloths & finished at the Mulstrum.
