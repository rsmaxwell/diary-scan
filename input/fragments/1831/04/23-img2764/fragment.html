A dull cold day & blowing fresh from NNE & sea coming on. Received a letter from WBS & answered it. Called at Chapmans, Mr Spence declines answering our note, but proposes continuing the loan on my giving a bill upon WBS to have his acceptance, the time to be afterward agreed upon when I secure WBS's answer. Walked up to the ship, all ready for sea, excepting wind. Towed her down to the low end of the town. Dined at home. On board the Mr Shand in the afternoon & at the Mulstrum in the evening.
