A beautiful morning but cool with a NE wind. Got a cask of rum on board the Hope. Ordered the men on shore as they refused to sign articles for less than £3 per month. Stephenson at Newcastle clearing.

Ministries in a minority of eight upon General Gascoyne's amendment on the reform bill respecting English members. Attended a meeting at the George tavern called in consequence of the minister being in the minority for the purpose of explaining their determination to support them in their proposed grievances & not to give any candidate a vote until he promises to support reform.

Finished at the Albion & Mulstrum.
