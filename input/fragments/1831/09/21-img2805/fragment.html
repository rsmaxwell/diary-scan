... decorated with all their orders, it was a very splendid & magnificent sight. I thought I should have been very nervous, but was not at all so. I was nominally presented by the Earl of Beverley, but in fact by the Lord in waiting who was I believe was Lord ____ Earl of Gosford I had the honour of kissing the Kings hand. Lord Beverley found me out in the ante room & was particularly polite & attentive. After leaving the Levee, WBS & Bob Marsh were waiting for me & we walked along Pall Mall & I got a coach & drove home where I exchanged my dress & returned to Richmond where I dined
