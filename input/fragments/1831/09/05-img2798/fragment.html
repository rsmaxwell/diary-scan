A fine morning. Wind SW & warm. Took the Paddington Stage into the city. Went to the office, nothing there, at the Coal Exchange. Saw there Brown & Fenwick. Walked out of the city. Called upon Bob Marsh, strolled round by Pall Mall, St James's Street & Piccadilly to Richmond's to dinner.

Got to Nottingham place at ½ past 10 o'clock & found WBS Mary & Lee had arrived.
