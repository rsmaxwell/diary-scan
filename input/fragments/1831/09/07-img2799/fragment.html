A dull unpleasant morning. Heavy showers all day. Wind SW. Took a walk & called upon Mr Lenskill & Mr Ward. Met Captain Shepherd in the Burley Tea Arcade who has got appointed to the HMS Melville 74 & going out to the East Indies with admiral Sir John Gore. Dined with Richmond at 6 o'clock to meet Mr & Mrs Hoffman alias Mary Fletcher, his sister & J Wrights
