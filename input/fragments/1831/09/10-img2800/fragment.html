Still damp unpleasant weather & appearance of more rain. Wind NW. Called a little earlier upon Lord Beverley who had again gone out. Returned to Nottingham Place. At 2 o'clock walked down to Charring Cross & there got upon a stage & went to the Elephant & castle & thence walked to George Harry Apollo Buildings, Walworth & saw Henry H. who I found laid up, being much bruised & injured by the upsetting of a Blackwell Stage. Returned by stage & dined at ½ past 5 o'clock with Richmond. Walked home early in the evening.
