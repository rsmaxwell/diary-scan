Been some rain this morning. Rather dull & excessively close & sultry. Wind for the ESE. Took an omnibus into the city. Called at the office. Accepted Stephenson's Wynberg bill for £44.5/- due 8 Nov payable with WBS & left it at Messrs Halmgons. Went down to the St Catherine dock & dined on board the Crisis. Tea at Lodlins. A heavy fall of rain with lightning at night.
