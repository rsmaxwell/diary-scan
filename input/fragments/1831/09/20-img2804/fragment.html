A very fine autumnal morning. Wind WNW. Been a little rain in the night. Took an omnibus into the city. At the offices. Called upon Messrs Harman's statement they had sent the Hope's Wyburn bill to Shields for above. Walked down to Miss Carr. Boswell gone down to Maldon this morning to the ship, which it appears, arrived there a fortnight ago, but he had not heard of it. Returned to the office & WBS & I walked out of the city together. Called at Bob Marsh's who afterwards joined is in Regents Street & went to Nottingham Place to dinner
