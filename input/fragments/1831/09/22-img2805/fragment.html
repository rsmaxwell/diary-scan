A fine morning. Wind NW. After breakfast WBS & I went into the city & at 11 o'clock embarked on board the Brocklebank steamer with an immense crowd of people & preceded to Woolwich to see the HMS Thunderer 74 a round ship launched. An immense concourse of people present. The King & Queen arrived about 2 o'clock & soon afterwards the Thunderer slid into the river in most beautiful style, the best launch I ever saw. Afterwards it was a grand & imposing sight. Returned to town & dined at the Blue Ports at 6 o'clock
