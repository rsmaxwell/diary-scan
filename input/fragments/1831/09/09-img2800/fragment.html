A very unpleasant day. Rained incessantly. Wind NW. Did not go out until 2 o'clock. Called upon Beverley who was not at home. Left my card. Walked down to Bob Marsh and Richmond's. Got a letter from Stephenson who will not be cleared so soon as expected. Answered it & told him I should not be at Hull & of my intention to leave town on Tuesday by the city of Edinburgh steamer. Called at Hawtry the dentists & got my teeth cleaned. Dined at 6 o'clock at Nottingham Place. Richmond & Bob Marsh dined with us.
