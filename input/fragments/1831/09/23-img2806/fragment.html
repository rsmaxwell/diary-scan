A beautiful morning. Wind about W by S. After breakfast walked into the city. At the office, received a letter from Bess B & one from Rob Stephenson by mistake, instead of Stephenson, Wyburn bill enclosing the merchant's bill and notes for the freight.

Paid Kitchenmein for repairing fishing rods £1.0.6.

Met H Harry & walked with him down to the Catherine docks to see Hy Nicholson who we found on board & had a lunch with him. His ship out exupting lathwood. He expects to get away about Wednesday & offered me a passage which I think I shall accept. Saw Messrs Harrison & explained Rolt's mistake in sending the freight bill instead of the Wyburn bill. Wrote to Bess. Walked back to Nottingham Place to dinner. Met WBS & Bob Marsh in Regent Street. The latter dined with us.
