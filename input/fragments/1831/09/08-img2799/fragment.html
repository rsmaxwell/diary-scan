A fine morning. Wind SW. The town all alive & on the move towards Charring Cross, Pall Mall etc. to see Coronation Procession. After breakfast I went out but could not see the procession, so great was the crowd. It appearing however to be near the United Services Club House after it had passed I saw major Falls who asked me in & upon I had a very fine view of the whole of it on its return. A most splendid & magnificent sight it was. Saw there Majesties very distinctly. Unfortunately there was a great deal of rain which they were in the Abbey which made the streets very dirty & dampt the pleasure of the people no doubt very much.
