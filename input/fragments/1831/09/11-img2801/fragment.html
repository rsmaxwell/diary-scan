A continuance of dull cloudy weather, wind NNW & every appearance of more rain. William, Emma & Lee went to church. I did not go being obliged to take some Cuberts. At 3 o'clock walked through the Regents Park to the Zoological Gardens with which & the collection of wild beasts. I was much pleased. Returned home & dressed & went to dine with Mr Leaskill at 7 o'clock, a small party, only Captain Greg & myself there
