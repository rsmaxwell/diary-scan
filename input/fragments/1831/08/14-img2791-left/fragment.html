A most beautiful morning. Wind from the N & E. Got up at 8 o'clock & breakfasted at 9 o'clock. From the noise & tremulous motion caused by the engine I got very little sleep. The boat off Scarborough at 3 o'clock in the morning & landed Mr J Brandling etc & passed Flamborough Head at 5 o'clock & the Dadgeon light about 1 o'clock. Dined at 3 o'clock & at the same time off Cromer. It was after 8 o'clock before we got through the Roads.
