An exceedingly fine morning. Wind SW. Wrote to Garbull & son of Hull to inform him when the Hope arrived etc. Dined at 2 o'clock at Mr lee's. The Lewis there & embarked at ½ past 5 o'clock on board the Ariadne steam boat at 6 o'clock. Sailed for Guernsey with a good many passengers on board. A most beautiful evening. Moon light & not a cloud to be seen. I walked the deck until we had passed the Needles & went to bed at 12 o'clock. R Marsh with us
