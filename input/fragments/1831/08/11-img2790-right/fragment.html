A beautiful day. Wind SW & ESE. Walked to Tynemouth. Ben Gailbraith went to Newcastle to see Emma. At Tynemouth again in the afternoon. Had a most delightful bath at the end of Cullercoats Sands & dined with Lieutenant Mitchel & Lockerwood at the castle. Bess met me on the mound, gave her a long lecture but query it has any good effect. Heavy shower of rain
