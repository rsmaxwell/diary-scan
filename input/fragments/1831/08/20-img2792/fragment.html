A fine day & blowing fresh from the WNW. I made an attempt to get out of bed this morning but my back was so bad I was obliged to lay down again. Breakfasted in bed. About 1 o'clock determined to make another attempt to get up & dress myself & with much difficulty & pain & the assistance of Ben I succeeded. After being up & walking a short time, found myself much easier. Walked down to the Pier & baths. Had a warm bath & Mr Lee afterwards drove me home in his gig. The remainder of the afternoon & evening found myself much relieved. About dinner time we had a very heavy squall with rain.
