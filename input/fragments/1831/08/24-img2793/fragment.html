A fine morning. Rather cloudy. Wind SW. Got up at 6 o'clock at which time we were abreast of the ____ with Alderney, Sark & Guernsey or St Peters Port in sight. Came to an anchor in Guernsey Sands about 8 o'clock & landed after a most delightful passage. The town of St Peter port is beautifully situated above the bay upon a rising hill & consists of a great many very excellent houses. It is of considerable extent but the streets are narrow. It however is rapidly improving and contains a great many beautiful private houses as well as public institutions, the collage the most striking ...
