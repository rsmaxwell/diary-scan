A dull morning having been rain in the night. Wind fresh at WNW. In endeavouring to throw my portmanteau upon my shoulder, it being very heavy, I strained the muscles of my back very severely & I fear I shall not soon recover from it. Pain very acute. Got on the coach at the White Horse ally at 8 o'clock with WBS & BBG & started for Southampton where we arrived at ½ past 4 o'clock having had very fine weather & a pleasant journey, the dust being laid by the rain. My back very painful & with some difficulty got down to my lodgings & to bed. We got Mrs Sands lodgings, the same as when at Southampton before.
