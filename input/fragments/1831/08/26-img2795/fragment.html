A continuance of fine weather. Amused ourselves by strolling about the markets etc. William went & saw Dr Proctor again, but I being tired did not go. Having settled our bill, at 2 o'clock we again embarked on board the Ariadne & sailed about 9 o'clock. The vessel most uncomfortably crowded, being about 155 passengers on board. We could not get berths. Passed close by the Island of Alderney. A fine sight.
