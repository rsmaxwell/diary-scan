A beautiful morning. Wind about N. After breakfast got upon a Paddington Stage & rode into the city to get my fishing rods. Surprised but could not find Kitchinman. The juaker, droked Lane having been cleaned away for the approach to London Bridge. Walked out of the city to the west end. Called upon & dined with Richmond. W Richmond & Miss Legg dinned with us. Got to Nottingham Place at 12 o'clock
