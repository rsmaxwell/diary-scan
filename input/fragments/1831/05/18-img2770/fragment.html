A delightful morning. Wind SE. Captain William Fanshawe Martin of the Samarang having hired a steamer I accompanied him & a party to Seaham with which we were much pleased and surprised at so vast an undertaking for one individual. Having taken one prog: etc with us we dined at the Prince & embarked again at ½ past 6 o'clock & reached Shields between 10 & 11 o'clock after having spent an uncommonly pleasant day. At the Mulstrum.
