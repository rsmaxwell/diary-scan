A very cold day. Wind NE. Received a letter from WBS enclosing our note for £300 at 6 months payable to Messrs Lees which I gave to Mr Spence. Went to Preston Main & his brother signed another lease for 3 years. Walked to Tynemouth & dined at 6 o'clock with Captain William Fanshawe Martin on board the Samarang frigate
