Another dull morning. The wind still from the old quarter NE. Walked to Tynemouth & saw the troops fire a salute at 12 o'clock in honour of the King's birthday. Returned & also saw the HMS Orestes gun brig do the same & man her yards. Dined at 4 o'clock with Mr Bradshaw in celebration of his wife's birthday, a very pleasant party & most splendid dinner & wines.
