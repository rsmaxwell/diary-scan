A dull orning & damp heavy been a good deal of rain in the night. Wind SE. Lord Howielt & Mr Beaumont came into the town conducted by an immense crowd of people with bands & colours, whom they addressed from a hustings erected in front on the Commercial. Turned out a very rainy afternoon. H Harry dined with us at the Mulstrum. In the evening gave Jonny Smith £28.1.6 Int for his mother.
