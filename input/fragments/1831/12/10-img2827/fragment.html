A raw cold morning. Wind from SW. Went by Dunns coach to Newcastle. At Bell & Browns ofein. The ship will get coals on Monday & be loaded on Wednesday. Saw Mr Anderson, Hope Club, to let him have the average papers on Tuesday. The committee having a meeting on Thursday to settle Hopes etc. Called & saw Emma Galbraith found her quite well. Returned to Shields by steam to dinner. At the billiard room & at night attended the whist club at H Harry's
