A fine morning & frosty. Wind WSW. Accepted a bill for balance of premium (182). Great Premium Clun £7.6.6 due 14 March. On board the Stokes, the remainder of her coals (4 keels) came down & she finished her loading. Dined at home. At the billiard room in the afternoon. The whist club in the evening. Called in at the widows at night & saw the Hope go to sea at 1 o'clock in the morning.

Wind WNW
