Fine weather. Wind from the WSW. Paid Hadawary Roper for a twice laid warp 82 fathoms at 28/- £7. Walked early out to Murton to see the Wallsend Harriers. Had but poor sport. Dined at 3 o'clock with Charlton at Billy Mill. Sat down 12 or 14 to an excellent col round of beef & roast potatoes, drank a great deal of wine, had a quarrel with Craig who behaved like a blackguard. Got to Bess at night.

Paid Red's Mother balance of ¼ years wages £2.9.3
