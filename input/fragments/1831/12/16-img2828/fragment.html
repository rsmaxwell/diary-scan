A fine morning. Wind WSW. Paid the Hope's piolet £1.16 & to 3 men for getting the ship chain hawser which she was obliged to ship & leave behind & for placing it on shore 7/6.

Gave a draft on Chapman's & Co for self £5

Dined at home. Joblings brothers with us.

At the theatre & with Bess at night.

In the evening blew a gale at SSE
