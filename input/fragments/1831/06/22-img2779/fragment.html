A fine morning. Wind SE. received a letter from Stephenson informing us of the ship's being released from quarantine on the Monday before she took the ground as she delivered in the old harbour at Mr Eade's warf. Bathed for the first time this season. Dined at home. H Harry, Richardson & I went over the water to Bullocks & had some peanalary. At the Albion in the evening & afterwards at the Mulstrum.
