A fine growing morning. Been a shower & the appearance of more rain. Wind SW.

Received a letter (post paid) from Harenan & Co, London, enclosing Stephenson's WyBurg bills of £50.5/ for acceptance. Accepted at by leave of Chapman's payable at Barketts & Co, dated 29th Aug & returned it by post. Lockwood dined with me. Walked round in the afternoon by the Doctor's garden to Tynemouth. At Tynemouth also in the morning. At the Albion & Mulstrum in the evening.
