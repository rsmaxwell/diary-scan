A fine morning blowing fresh at SW by S. Received a letter from Stephenson of his safe arrival at Hull after a fine passage of sixteen days from Wynburg (Cape Town). He has to lay 8 days quarantine.

Paid average Hope Club £9.17.6 & Mrs Brown sail-making etc. £5.15.6. Wrote to Stephenson. Dined at home. Went on board William C Wright's ship the Mulgrave Castle with H Harry, bound to Archangel. At the Albion & afterwards at the Mulstrum
