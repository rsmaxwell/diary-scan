A dull & very sultry close morning. Wind N by W & NNW. Called upon Martin Orr at the Garrison. Saw Mr Garbutt but declined fixing a foreign mail being expected & the chance of freights being better. H French dined with me on board after having a long walk about the town. The ship out & cleared this afternoon. Wrote to Bess B. Martin Orr & his wife called upon me at the ship. Set H French part of the way home & amused myself the remainder of the evening by strolling round the town. Turned out a very rainy day.
