A fine morning. Wind about West. Gave Roberts a draft for £5 for the house. Got £5 of Chapmans on my own account. H Harry & I took the steamer to Newcastle & walked up to the course. The two last races very well contested. Very little company there ended & left from there usual. Dined at the Grand Stand. Walked into Newcastle again & returned to Shields which we did not reach until 11 o'clock having been agreed at St Peter's & 3 ½ hours in coming down. At the Mulstrum
