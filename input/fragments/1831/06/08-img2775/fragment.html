A dull cloudy morning. Wind NW. Glass fallen & more the appearance of rain. Received a letter from Emma Galbraith informing me of the holy days commencing on the 17th. But glad to see her so much improved in her writing. Walked to Tynemouth. Dined at home. At Tynemouth again & Cullercoat Sands in the afternoon & evening. At the Albion.

Towards noon the wind got into the old quarter NE again & turned out a most beautiful day.
