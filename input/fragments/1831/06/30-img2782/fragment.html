Finer weather. Wind NNW. Breakfasted with Orr & his wife at the Garrison. Saw the armoury containing the arms of all the local militias etc of the district, about 20000. Walked up to the ship, a lighter up with 40 tons of ballast. At Garbutt's, Wade not having got the deal counted are unable to settle the freight. Dined on board. Walked about the town & docks in the afternoon & spent the evening with Orr. Wrote to WBS
