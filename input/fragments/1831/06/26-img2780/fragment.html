A dull morning but fair. Wind fresh at N by E & NNE. Crossed the water & got on the coach for York westside at 8 o'clock where we arrived at 6 o'clock to dinner after a very pleasant drive without rain. Got an inside in the march at ½ past 7 o'clock & arrived at Hull at ½ past 12 o'clock. A beautiful moonlight night. Drank a glass of whiskey Toddy & went to bed.
