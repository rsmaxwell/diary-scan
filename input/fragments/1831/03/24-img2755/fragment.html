A fine morning but still cold. Wind NE. Attended morning drill in the pasture. Marched round with my company for the first time. Marched to the pasture again in the afternoon at 2 o'clock marched past slow & quick time & went through the manoeuvres. The two Richards & I went in chaise to dine with Mr Burrell BroomPark. Spent a pleasant day & got home at 2 o'clock.
