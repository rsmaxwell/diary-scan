A fine morning. Wind S by W. Received a letter from Captain Crisp adjutant of the regiment stating the Colonel Coulson having received a notification from the Duke of Northamptonshire that my commission was preparing & that I must order my uniform etc & hold myself in readiness to join. Attended a meeting at the North aems, the room full, to petition both houses of parliament to pass the reform bill & setting forth our approval of the measure. Went up to the ship. Dined at home. Wrote to WBS giving him a copy of the above mentioned letter. At the Albion in the evening.
