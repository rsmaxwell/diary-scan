A very fine day. Wind NNE. Called & left my card at Mr Carr's, Bondgate hall.

Mr Potts having offered me a seat in his carriage, we all lunched together at 1 o'clock at Mr Spousers. The ladies having previously been to see the castle. Started between 2 & 3 o'clock & reached Mr Joblings in Newcastle between 7 & 8 o'clock. Had two glasses of spirits & water which screwed me. Got a gig & reached Shields between 9 & 10 o'clock. Called at the Mulstrum & stayed there till 4 o'clock in the morning.
