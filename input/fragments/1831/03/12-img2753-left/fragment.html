A fine morning and wind SW. Received a letter from Alnwick from the adjutant Crisp ordering me to join the Regiment. Walked to Tynemouth with the intention of taking a warm bath, but they are not yet open for the season. Returned & went up to the Hope. Repairs nearly finished. At high water they hauled her off the shore & dropped her down to Custom House Quay. Toward the afternoon it began to blow very hard varying between SSW & SSE & increased to a gale which continued all night. Spent the evening at home writing etc.
