... my company a present of 1/- to each man, 2/ to the corpral & 21/- to the three sergeants:

Got my clothes etc all packed up & sent them home by Ellcots the Warkworth carrier. Dined at the mess & being the last we spent a very pleasant & jolly day all regretting that we were to separate but hoping that we shall soon be personally called out.

Robert rode one of Mr Potters horses home.
