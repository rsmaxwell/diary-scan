A very fine morning. Wind S by W. Got up early & took gig to Newcastle. Joined the Alnwick coach at 10 o'clock & arrived there 3 o'clock. Found a box with by blue susloop etc at the White Swan. Got a billet at the Star with Potts & two Reeds. Reported myself to the adjutant. Dined at the Keep at 6 o'clock & in the evening went to the Theatre with Major Ogle. A very cold raw day. Heavy rain in the afternoon.
