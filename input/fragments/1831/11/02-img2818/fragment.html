Rather a dull morning. Wind fresh from SW & little rain.

Sent Stephenson up to & he settled with Mr Archer. Received 30/0 & a deal plank. The ship got her beef on board. Sent WBS a round up. Walked up to Tynemouth. Saw Rifles march for Manchester. Dined at home at 5 o'clock. Lockerwood came in & took a glass of toddy with me.

With Bess in the evening
