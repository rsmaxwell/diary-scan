A beautiful morning. Air frosty & glass high. Wind NW.

Received a letter from Wilkinson & Angus saying they could not purchase the carriage.

Walked with Boswell to Tynemouth. Jobling arrived & agreed to stay with me. He dined with me at 2 o'clock. At the billiards room in the afternoon & with Bess at night.

Ordered 2 gallons of whiskey for one of which Jobling paid.
