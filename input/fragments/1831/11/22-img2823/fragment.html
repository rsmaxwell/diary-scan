A mild day. Wind about west. Walked to Tynemouth with Boswell. Received a letter from Messrs Hurry & co. The coals being all out on Saturday & that they had found the leak to be in an open trennail hole between the timber under the starboard for chain about two feet below the light water mark. Had plugged it up & after heaving down & driving a new trennail, expected she would be able to return to Shields without the necessity of being docked at Yarmouth.

Dined at home. At the billiard room in the afternoon & with Bess at night.
