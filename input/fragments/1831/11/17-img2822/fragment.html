A sharp frost in the night having been a thaw yesterday. The streets this morning a sheet of ice & dangerous walking. Wind WNW. The clubs received a letter from Messrs Hurry's stating they had sold the remainder of the coals to be taken out of the ship in two days. Wrote to WBS & sent the Hairmongers protest with a copy of Chapman's book & an account of rents. Attended the whist club. Boswell came home with us in the evening to join us in a glass of toddy. Took physic
