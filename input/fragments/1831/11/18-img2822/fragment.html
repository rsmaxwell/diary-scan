A fine frosty morning. Wind WNW. Having from cold I suppose, the back of my neck & head much affected & sore. I this morning took a black draught & lay in bed till 1 o'clock. Charles Redley called from Hartley. Dined & spent the afternoon with us. He left us at ¼ past 9 o'clock to walk back being a fine moonlight night. I went along & slept with Bess. Robert yesterday paid Philipson Mason his bill £37 & today paid into Chapman's £15 more rents
