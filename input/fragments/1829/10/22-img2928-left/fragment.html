A dull morning after a very heavy fall of rain during the night. Wind NW. After breakfast Kit & Joe went to see the Lambleton hounds throw off at Flags & James, Kits servant, drove me in the gig to Durham, where I had to remain all day, the coach not passing through till evening. Showers during the whole of the morning, but in the intervals I strolled about to see all my old haunts etc. Things much in a "status quo". I could perceive few alterations or improvements. Called upon SKeene. At 8 o'clock took a gig & got to shields at ½ past 9 o'clock & went to the whist club for an hour. Received a letter from William.
