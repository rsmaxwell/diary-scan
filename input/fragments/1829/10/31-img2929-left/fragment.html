A fine morning. Rather cool. Wind changeable between NNW & NNE. Sea high, having blown very hard early this morning from NE. Emma Galbraith came to spend the day with Mrs Scott. Took a walk with her round by Preston Lane etc. Dined at 5 o'clock with Allen, J Craig, J Wright, W Harrison & Robinson of the 80th there. Spent a pleasant day & won of W Harrison £6
