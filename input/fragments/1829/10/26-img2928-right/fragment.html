Another beautiful morning. Wind about West. Went over the water with H Harry to see Mr S Walkers patent lever to the old windlass worked, but it was unshipped. Wrote to C Kirkmond about the price of mineral timber. Mrs Dixon down to see Mrs Scott, rather more gracious than before. She invited me to Benton.

Dined at home. Drank a couple of tumblers of whiskey toddy with Allen & went to the theatre to see Madam Vestris perform Latitea Harog in the Billes Shalagen & Apollo in Midas. A pretty good house, about £37 in
