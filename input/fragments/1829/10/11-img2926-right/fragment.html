Another beautiful mild day. Wind about West. Got up this morning. Very stiff and aching from the exercise of yesterday. Mr Richmond, Joe & I walked to Lanchester Church. Heard the service & saw the natives. Returned home by the Greenwells of the Ford. Saw Mr White & family of Woodlands at church, lops kindly invited us to dine with him next week. Dined at 3 o'clock without company. Took a stroll after our wine, spent a quiet evening & got to bed early
