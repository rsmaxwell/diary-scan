A very stormy morning. Exceptional wet & heavy sleet showers. Wind about N. Got breakfast early & took the steam boat to Newcastle & at 10 o'clock got into the Express coach & went to Durham. When we arrived a little after 12 o'clock Richmond had sent his man and gig for me but by some mistake the man supposing I had not come, returned to Burnhopeside without me at ½ past 12 o'clock. I therefor started & walked out to dinner.
