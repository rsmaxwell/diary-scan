A dull morning. After a rainy night & wind NE, changed now to NNW. During the day variable between W by N & NNE and cold. A sea upon the bar. Amused myself drawing all the morning. Dined at home. Received from Robert £7 on WBS account for newspapers sent Commercial Rooms & £1.18.6 on account of Emma Galbraith's.

The Grenville Bay & Love arrived from Greenland well fisked. H Harry & I had a bottle of port together at the George. With Ann Stokes in the evening
