A very wet day. Still raining, very heavy. Wind I think NNW. Walked to Burnhopeside to breakfast. Bressed & Joe Reshar, Allen Hutchinson & I went with Mr Potts, who came for us in his carriage to Mr Whites at Woodlands where we dined & spent a very pleasant & jolly day & after supper returned to Burnhopeside. Kit & Miss R stayed all night at Mr Whites
