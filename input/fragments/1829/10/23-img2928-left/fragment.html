A dull misty morning. Wind NW & N by W

Accepted balances bill at 4 months for the Mesa at premium £20-2.6 due 20th Jan

Went to South Shields & paid Rids & Oliver their dividend upon G Morrisons estate. Dined at house. Wrote & enclosed a soverign to Mrs Scott Crathorn. H Harry & I drank wine together at the Commercial & afterwards eat some oysters at Janes
