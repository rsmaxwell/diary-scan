A dull morning. Wind NE. Afterwards turned out pleasant. Captain Webb & I walked to Whitley Park & called upon Mr J Wright. Returned by Cullercoats & the sands through Tynemouth. Dined at 2 o'clock. At the Mallard soon in the afternoon. At ½ past 7 o'clock went to meet Ann but she was not at the place of appointment. Drank tea & spent the evening with Miss Tinley. Playing at cards with the boys. Wind changed. Rained in the evening to SE
