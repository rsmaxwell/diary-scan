A damp morning, having been a great deal of rain during the night. Wind NNW. Mrs Scott very ill. Wind variable during the day between NNW & N by E. At church in the morning. The newspapers countenancing the speeding in Parliaments of the news as to declaring their intention to grant Catholic emancipation which caused a great sensation here, but particularly to the incomcas to it in both houses of parliament. Dined at J Tinley's. The two Carrs there.
