A beautiful morning & hard frost. Wind W & W by S. A French & I walked round the town with Mr Chapman of Elseneur to call upon his friends. Dined at 2 o'clock with A Harry. On board the Choise, A French, A Nicholson, Drewing, Cunningham, Mark ____, self & Captain ____ Mr Bradshaw came on board. After dinner we spent a very pleasant day.
