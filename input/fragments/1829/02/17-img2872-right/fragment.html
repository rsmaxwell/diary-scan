A damp morning it having been a great deal of rain & sleet during the night. Wind NNW changeable during the day to ENE & NE. Went down to the ship. The committee on board surveying stores for the Hope club. Dined at J Tinleys with a party of gentlemen. We all afterwards went to the ball. Only 52 there, but a pleasant dancing ball. Ordered a new mainstaysail for the Hope.
