A fine morning. Wind NW. Went by coach to Newcastle. Called at D Dixons. Found Mrs Dixon about to set off for Shields to see Mrs Scott. Called at G Dunn & Sons & refused to load their coals without being upheld. Afterwards walked down to Shields. Dined at Mrs Reeds with a large party. The bridal party Mr & Mrs Tom Wright there. Attended the whist club at A Metcalf. Paid R Brown the account £4.10/-
