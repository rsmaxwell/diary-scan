A cold morning. Wind SW. Has been some rain in the night. Received a letter from the committee of the Hope Club ordering us to get a new lamp & next winter a new foresail. Also now four new ballast shovels. Engaged all the morning writing to William & the Galbraiths. Found Mrs Craig in a chaise & went to Walls to dine with Mr J Wright, a large party of 18. Spent a pleasant day & had the best port wine I ever tasted in my life. Got home at 12 o'clock.
