A fine frosty day & sharp frost. Wind W by S. Engaged part of the morning drawing. Took a walk round by the ship. Found all right, Stephenson on board waiting for Mr Cleths to survey her for the Premium Club. Dinned with Mr Chapman of Elseneur at the Commercial. A French & W Boswell came and took wine. Went to the theatre, Crooks benefit, a fair house& the last night of performance for the season. Got £5 of Chapman for self.
