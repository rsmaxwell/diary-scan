A fine morning. A hard frost. Wind E by S. Has been a little snow in the night. Last night the mews room was on fire but was discovered in time to get put out before much damage was done. In a short time it would broken out & spread that the whole building would have been destroyed. The floor rafters and ceiling of mews room partly burnt. Walked with Boswell to Tynemouth. Dined at J Dales. Met a large party & spent a pleasant day.
