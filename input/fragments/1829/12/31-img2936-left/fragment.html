The wind continuing from the SE makes it appear as if there was not much if any frost. Though some snow has fallen during the night.

The last day of the year, which has been to me anything but a prosperous one. I trust I may be more fortunate the next one. I know not what will become of me. It appears to me my destiny is fixed & that I'm never to rise in the world again.

Walked to Cullercoats & saw the fishing boats come in having been successful. The fish very firm, particularly the haddock. There were 16 boats. Dined at home. Attended the whist club & sat the new year in.
