A foggy damp and very unpleasant morning. Some snow on the ground, having fallen during the night. Wind, the little there is NNW. Dined at 4 o'clock at Mr Metcalf's in Saville Street. A party there. Spent a pleasant day. Wrote to William. Called at the Bank. Spencer promised to under me what accommodation I may want this Xmas. Paid Linskill & etc £24.11.6 for cordage & Lane & Spence & Foster £8.10/- for canvas
