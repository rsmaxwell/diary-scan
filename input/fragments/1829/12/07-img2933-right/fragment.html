Fine weather, rather frosty. Wind SW & SSW. The Hope arrived safe last night at 11 o'clock. Determined upon laying her up & therefore took her off risk in the Premium & Hope Clubs. Gave Stephenson £20 to pay wages. The William Shand, Mr Boswell sailed today for Ryes to take on passengers for New York. A great many colliers also got to sea. Dined with the Committee of the ____ & in the evening went to the theatre, it being the bespeak of the Bachelors of North Shields.
