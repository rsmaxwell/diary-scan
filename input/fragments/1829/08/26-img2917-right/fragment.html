By daylight this morning. They got the ship underway at low water. When we got up at 8 o'clock the weather much pleasanter & continued fine all day. At noon brought up at high water abreast of Whitaker Beason. Wind SW. At 5 pm, low water, we again got under way, but were much delayed by a ship giving us a foul birth, the consequence was it became dark before we could work through Shoe hole & see the mouse buoy, where unfortunately it also turned very squally & blowing a gale of wind.
