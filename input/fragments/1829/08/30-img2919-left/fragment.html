Got up much refreshed. More moderate weather. Wind NE rather cloudy. We fully intended to have gone to church, but Bob & I took up so much linin in the washing & cleaning ourselves that it was past the hour before we were aware of it. Turned out a very fine day. We called upon Mr Ward who had gone to Paris will William. Walked through St James park up to Hyde Park where we strolled till 6 o'clock. Very few people or carriages there. Town exceedingly thin from thence came we went to Mr Piazza Covent garden & enjoyed a comfortable dinner & a good bottle of old port. Returned to Chambers between 10 & 11 o'clock & went to bed.
