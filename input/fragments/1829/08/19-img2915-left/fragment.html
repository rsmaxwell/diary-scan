This morning thick weather the land in sight like a fog bank, but clearing away we found it for certain to be Huntcliff foot, bearing west about five miles. During the morning light wind variable & in the afternoon calm with tremendous heavy rain, thunder & lightning. At night the wind being from the NE light breeze & I very unwell with scumalign. The ship short of Stocks as water coals & pump gear out of order & considering she might reach shields, by daylight, we determined to work to windward & turned in for the night.
