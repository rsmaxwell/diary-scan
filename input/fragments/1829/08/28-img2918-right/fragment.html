... were compelled to give the ship the whole chain. Wind WNW & cloudy. Dismal weather with rain. A light ship close by us drove & another broke his chain & sun down the river. Continued blowing hard all day with heavy showers of rain & squalls & towards evening the wind got to about N by W but the tide not answering. Till near dark, we lay still, indeed it blew too hard to think of getting under way & one or two vessels that weighed were soon obliged to bring up again
