Turned out this morning between 5 & 6 o'clock found the ship turning to windward off Marsden Rock blowing hard from N by E. The wind having increased during the night with a high sea. At about 9 o'clock, the breeze increasing & no chance of us reaching Tynemouth of getting on shore.
