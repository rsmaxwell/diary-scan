Another beautiful day. The ship making good way about 4 ½ knots with a fine breeze at SSE going free. We can perceive the Islands of Ertholmene & Bornholm with the glass. The ship much to my annoyance very leaky. Obliged to pump every hour. At 1 o'clock pm passed close to Ertholmene. With a fine breeze at S by E. Studding sails set. These are small rocky islands belonging to Denmark about 11 miles from Bornholm. Upon the largest is a lighthouse, a few small batteries & houses. On the south side a very good harbour I understand.
