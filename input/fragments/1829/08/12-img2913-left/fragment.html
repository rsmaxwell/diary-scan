Spent a miserable night, having had little or no sleep. Looked out about 5 o'clock. The gale as heavy as ever & without any appearance of change, although the sky very clear. At 11 o'clock Masskand Castle in sight distant about 4 leagues, when we determined no longer to contend with the winds but bore away for Gottenburg & in the course of half an hour those wise few that did not follow our example at ½ past 3 o'clock we reached Wings sound & before 5 o'clock brought up in the harbour of Warga, a small channel between two docks, rejoicing with the prospect of obtaining a good nights rest.
