Turned out this morning at the usual hour. Coppers hot & rather feverish, took a seidletz, very pleasant answering a double purpose. A beautiful morning. Summer returned. A light breeze at SE. At noon by observation in lat 55.10N. Kept our reach the whole of the day. Wind NW. At 8 o'clock tacked ship. Reigen Island bearing S by W distant 10 or 12 miles.
