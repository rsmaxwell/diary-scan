Turned out after a food nights rest at 8 o'clock. The gale as heavy as ever with rain. (Sent top gallant yards driven & struck the mast)

At 11 o'clock finer weather & some appearance of clearing up having had a glimpse of the sun.

Wrote a letter to William & Mr Clark of the Duke of Clarence coming on board. We all went on shore at 12 o'clock to put the letter into the post. Walked about & saw a little of the town of Yarmouth with which I was much pleased. After having some bread & cheese, portion & a glass of grog, we left the jetty about 4 o'clock ...
