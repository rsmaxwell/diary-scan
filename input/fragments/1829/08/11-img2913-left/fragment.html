What with the howling of the wind, the rolling & tossing of the ship & creaking of tallow casks besides being leaky & upon a lee shore, we had spent a most uncomfortable day. Did not attempt to go to bed, indeed mine was so damp I could not, but I lay down upon the captains about midnight all standing
