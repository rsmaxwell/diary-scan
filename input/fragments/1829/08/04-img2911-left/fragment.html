A delightful change. Now beautiful weather. Wind NW & W. Light breeze & sea very much fallen, only going about 2 knots. At 12 o'clock by observation at lat:55.47N. Employed myself all the morning in preparing the wood for a dog kennel. At 2 o'clock pm the wind got to the WSW course NW. At 5 pm set studding sails, top masts & main top gallant. Sea quite fallen & a fine fresh breeze at 6 o'clock wind S course W by S going 5 knots at 10 o'clock discharged our grog & turned in for the night, ship going 5 ½ knots
