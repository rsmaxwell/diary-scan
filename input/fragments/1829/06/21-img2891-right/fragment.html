Turned out at ½ 7 o'clock. Found the ship still in sight of Falsterbo, having been working to windward all night & made but little progress, wind fast. After breakfast read the service & lessons of the day. Just before I got upon deck, the XYZ passed us homeward bound, with a fine breeze & studdingsails set. We continued working to westward the whole of the day. At 9 o'clock took our gin & water & went to bed.
