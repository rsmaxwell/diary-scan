... The slightest air possible during the whole of the day & making such little way that we were enabled to catch a couple of coldlings by our lines going near the bottom. At 6 o'clock pm a slight breeze sprung up from the NNE which run us pretty near against the north end of Bornholm, where at 9 o'clock it suddenly changed to against us, but died away to a dead calm. Had our whisky & water & turned in. Bornholm a very fine island as sun with the glass.
