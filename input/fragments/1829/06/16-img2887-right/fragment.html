Got up a little after 7 o'clock. Had a fresh breeze all night from the southward. The ship making 8 knots per hour. A continuance of most beautiful weather. At 12 o'clock took observation Lat:57.4 long: 7.17 having made 101 miles in the last 24 hours, making 284 since our departure. Employed part of the morning in painting the skiff.
