Turned out at ½ past 7 o'clock & most delightful morning, with a fine breeze from NW. been going 5 & 6 knots all night. After breakfast going 6 knots & steering an ENE course. At few of our companions, the dragon flies still with us. At 12 o'clock took observation & by it, with in lat:56.36. long: 18.41
