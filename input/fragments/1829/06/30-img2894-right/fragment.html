Got up at 8 o'clock. A beautiful morning & exceptionally hot. Found a great many Shields ships laying in the mole amongst them the Gordeson, Cholmley, the Wellington, H Daeghton & & Bob Marsh & I went on board & had our breakfast with H Deyghtow & his wife. The master & J Galbraith went on shore early to Mr Booker to report the ship & vansack
