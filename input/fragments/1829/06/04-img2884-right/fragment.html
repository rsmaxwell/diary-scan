Got up at 11 o'clock. Breakfasted at the Fountain & saw Ann safe to her aunty. Walked into the city. Called at the country house. William gone. Returned to Nottingham Place with Mr Lee. Went with the Lees to dine with A Patterson at Clapton, a party. A very fine but extremely hot day. Wind WNW & NW. Received a letter from Mr Vernon of J Galbraith setting off for London.
