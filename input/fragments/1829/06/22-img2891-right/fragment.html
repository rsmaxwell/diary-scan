A continuance of fine weather but wind still from the east & rather cool. Towards the afternoon the wind gradually decreased & became much warmer, so that we are in hope it will die away & shall have a change. This working to windward in the Baltic is much more tiresome than anywhere else. The current also being always against you. In the evening became quite calm. At 9 o'clock took our tumbler of whisky toddy & went to bed. The Swedish coast very fine & pretty, well wooded.
