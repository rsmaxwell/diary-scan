A dull morning. Wind NW. A good deal of rain after a breakfast. Walked into the city. Brought a filtering machine for the ship. Gave Stephenson £25. Settled the freight account with William & received the balance £30.4/-. Called at the Russian consul for passports. Dined at Richmonds. Hard J Jobling Chambers & there. When I got home, found J Gilbraith had arrived.
