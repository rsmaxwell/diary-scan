... set top gallent studding sail. Fine weather. The ship in excellent run & passing everything.

Saw the lighthouse on the island of Auholt bearing W by S distant about 8 miles. At this time we could not see the land. Cloud being exceedingly low

At 9 o'clock wind NW by N a fair breeze & going about 5 knots. The Koll land in sight bearing S by E distant 15 miles.

Took our glass of grog as usual & went to bed
