Turned out at ½ past 6 o'clock. Found that J Galbraith had kept his watch last night for the first time. Have had a fair breeze all night from the SW. Continuing our course NE with steering sail set at the rate of 6 knots. Got fishing line out but could not catch nothing. The ship going too fast through the water.

Bob & I employed ourselves in painting the skiff & making her decent to go to shore in at Elsinecra.

At 12 o'clock the Captain took his observation lat 55.1 long: 4.15 having been 112 miles since 12th yesterday & 123 from our departure.
