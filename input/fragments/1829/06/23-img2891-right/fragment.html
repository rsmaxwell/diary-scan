Turned out before 8 o'clock & most beautiful morning & although calm during the night, the sea so smooth, that the ship with the little air there was, has, as the Captain terms it, been sliding through the water. To my surprise, found Bornholm in sight. The north of the island bearing east distant about 10 miles SE by E which is about an hour changed to SSW. Set foretopmast & maintop gallant studding sails.
