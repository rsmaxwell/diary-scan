A beautiful day. Rather frosty. Wind NNW. The sea still continues heavy upon the bar. Attended morning services.

Received a letter from WBS & another from Captain Stephenson dated aneruel 17th Oct & expecting to sail next day. London on the 15th drawn upon Chapmans for expenses £93.4/- which I consider heavy.

Dined at home at 2 o'clock & afterwards took a walk to Tynemouth with H Harry. Mrs Scott not so well. Indeed the Dr considering her getting considerably worse. The disease having extended to the armpits.

At the Mulstrum.
