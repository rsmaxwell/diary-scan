A rainy morning. After a stormy night. Wind variable N by W. N & NE & a heavy sea upon the bar. Wrote to WBS & sent him an account of rents. Received a letter from him of the arrival of the Hope at Gravesend. Dined at 5 o'clock at J Tinley's. William & H Metcalf, H Dale, Mr Gosling, R A J. Wright jn & Mr Duncan from Liverpool there. A very pleasant party.
