A fine mild morning with the appearance of rain. Wind WSW. Dined at the George Tavern with William Harrison who had last a diciseen upon the death of Major Wergs. He (WH) saying that the major was killed at Corunna when it was at St Sabastian's. Had an exceedingly pleasant day & excellent dinner & wine. Present J Tinley, Captain French, Mr West & Mr Robinson of the 80th Regiment, Mr Allen, William, Tom & Benj Wright, I Craig, Mr Christie of London, Mr Harrison & myself
