A fine morning, but rather dull. Wind NW. Took physic & could not go to church. Wrote to Mr P Lacing respecting C Harry's claim. At the grange man Sunderland. Dined at home. The newspapers today filled with Mr Puls expositions of the measures of ministise for the relief of the Catholics. Took wine in the evening with H Harry & J Lunning have at the Albion. Afterwards called at the Mulstrum & saw Bell Jarvis !!!!
