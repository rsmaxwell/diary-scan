A damp morning. Showers of rain & blowing fresh from SSW. Attended a meeting of the parishioners at the church respecting the burial ground at Tynemouth. The board of ordinance having given orders that no more interments shall take place. A most arbitrary & unjust proceeding & which the parish considers they have no right to do, it having been the parish church & ground for time immemorial. Dined with Mr Allen & afterward went on board the Hope. Got six more keels on board today. In the evening at the whist club. Paid Geo Slops £1.13/- the balance of years wages account.
