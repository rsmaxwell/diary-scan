A fine morning & blowing fresh from the SW & cold. Another large fleet coming in. Borrowed off Robert £5. On board the Hope. Two keels alongside. People engaged lending sails. Walked up to the spout/huroonaschs & took lunch on board the 3 Brothers with A French, H Harry, Allen, W Harrison, J Wright there. Dined with J Tinley a party & in the evening went to the ball which was thinly attended but a very pleasant one.
