Weather moderating and fair having been very strong all night. Wind about E by S. Several ships arrived this morning. Among them the Hope having left London on Tuesday. A schooner loaded with grain got upon the hard Sand, the sea higher than has been this winter. Went to Newcastle & fixed the Hope to load Townty main again the price 20/ chat & to be applied 8/6 freight. Returned by steam to dinner. Wind changed to SW & then to SE.
