Rather a dull morning. Wind blowing fresh from SW & W. Several ships sailed for America but no loaded vessels got out. A heavy sea being upon the bar. Moved the Hope from the crane to a tees in South Shields. The river on the north side being so crowded Dined at home. Joined a party on board the Harmony. Mr Peark in the evening. With Bell Jarvis. Paid into Chapmans. The bill for £90.10/-
