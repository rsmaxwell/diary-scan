A very wet morning. Wind SW. On board the Hope. She got two keels down today making 15. Dined on board. Dr Drewery & W Peart with me. A great many ships got to sea this afternoon. The wind changed to S & S by E. Poor R Brown died today. A serious loss to his family. Could not attend the oyster supplier at the George in consequence. Gave Goe Slops 20/- on account of his wages.
