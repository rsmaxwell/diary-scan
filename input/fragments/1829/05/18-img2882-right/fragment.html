At 3 o'clock am got under way & sailed from Shields with a westerly breeze & fine weather. At 10 o'clock am becalmed off Sunderland, the wind having got round to SE. Between 1 & 2 o'clock the breeze freshened a little but we made little way, being ebb tide. At 10 o'clock we went to bed, the ship being abreast of the Tees
