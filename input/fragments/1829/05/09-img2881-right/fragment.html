A fine spring morning. Some showers during the night. Warmer growing weather at last. Wind W by N. Mr Fergusson & I went to Newcastle by steam on the quay, but could do nothing for the Hope. Called upon Inspector Hodgson & dined with him at his brothers. Returned to Shields in the evening. With Bell Jarvis, remained with her until 4 o'clock in the morning. Received from Geo Dunn & sons £27 being the difference between the freight made & that upheld =rig: 8/6
