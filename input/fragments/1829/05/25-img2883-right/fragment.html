A fine morning, but blowing hard from the N by E. William & I walked into the city. Called upon Bob Marsh only a short time at the office, when Stephenson brought Ann up. I walked with her to Grays Inn, showed her St Pants, went round by Oxford & Regents street & Ann feet being very sore we after buying a pair of easy shoes, took a coach & drove to new inn. Dined with Bob Marsh & afterwards we all went to the pit at Covent garden Theatre. Ann much astonished at the size & splendour of the houses. We saw Romeo & Juliet performed by C Kenneth & Mrs Smithson. Then afterwards the Devils Elixir, not worth seeing. Ann & I slept at the Fountain in Catherine Street
