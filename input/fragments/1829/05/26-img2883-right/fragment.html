A beautiful day. Wind still fresh from the N by E. We got breakfast at 11 o'clock & afterwards walked into the Green park round by the Kings Place by Pall Mall to Somerset House & saw the exhibition. Ann much pleased with her mornings amusements took & left in Grays Inn. Walked into the city. Attended Changes, did nothing with the ship & walked to Nottingham Place to dinner. The Hope could not be sold today.
