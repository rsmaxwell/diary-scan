Got up about 7 o'clock. A most delightful morning. Wind unfortunately changed to W so that at the early morning tide, we could not move, There also being very little of it. After breakfast dressed myself & landed at Gravesend about 11 o'clock, waited until about 1 o'clock before I could get a coach. Got to town at 4 o'clock pm. Left the ships papers at the office & got to Nottingham Place at ½ past 5 o'clock. Found them all well & glad to see me. Bob Marsh & his father dined with us. Heavy rain in the afternoon.
