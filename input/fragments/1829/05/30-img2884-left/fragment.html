Another cloudy morning. Wind NE. Walked into the city. Called upon Bob Marsh. He & his father walked with me down to the Hope. Saw the St Catherine Docks in our way & the most perfect & comfortable perhaps of thing of the sort all the warehouses & upon so good a place. Had lunch on board & I afterwards attended changes but did nothing for the ship. Walked out of the city with Inspector Chapman & dined with him. His brothers the captain, Thomas, Ben, Mr Graham & a Mr Day there.
