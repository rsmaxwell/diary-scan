A fine morning. Wind NE. Paid into Chapmans £27 the balance of sales & our ship Hope for £69.5/-. Went to Newcastle again & fixed the Hope to load Gamlington Walls at 9/6 freight by spout clear of brokerage & the freighters to pay two keel dues should she not load up. Dined on board the Choice with H Harry & A French. Wrote to William. The Hope dis lorry is to go past him to millis & roth
