A beautiful summers morning. Wind ENE. Received a letter from William agreeing to offer Younger 10/- in the £. Drew £20 out of Chapman. £15 for Stephenson & £5 for self. Drew £5 more in the afternoon. Stephenson wanting £20. Dined at home. In the evening called upon A Dale to propose to join Younger's 10/- in full. Dale sacfe for Younger, who I afterwards found much to my relief of mind had agreed to our proposal. Spent the evening with Mr Allen. With Bell Jervis.
