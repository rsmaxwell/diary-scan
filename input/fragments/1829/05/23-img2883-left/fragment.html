At 3 o'clock [in] the morning they got the ship under way, with a light breeze from the NE. At 7 o'clock I got up & went upon deck, past through a large fleet of light ships. At 8 o'clock got the flood with a little more wind. Again passed a great number of light ships. Between 5 & 6 o'clock brought up a little blow Gravesend. The crew went to bed early. Ann & I walked the deck until after 10 o'clock. Sat up until 12 o'clock. Went to bed to Ann
