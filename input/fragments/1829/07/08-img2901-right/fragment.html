... the Petershoff Road, but being plagued with my toothache & my jaw much swollen, I was obliged to let Bob go by himself & dined at the club with Mr Lindsey. He & I in the evening each got a horse of Me Webster who keeps excellent cattle & took a very pleasant ride down by Catharinoff & round home by the Petershoff Road. After having our grog, retired to bed between 11 & 12 o'clock, exceedingly stiff & fatigued.
