... took a walk in the summer gardens of the Palace. On our return had a glass of grog with Mr Lindsey & got to bed at 12 o'clock.

Got admission into the premises where they are building the new Isaac Church & now engaged in erecting the granite pillars for the Portico & they use of an immense size most beautifully polished. The finest & longest in the world, brought from the quarries in Finland, their circumference about 30 feet & height upwards of 50 & although they weight ___ tons, they raise them in 20 minutes
