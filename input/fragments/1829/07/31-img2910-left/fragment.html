Fine weather and light breezes from WNW. During the whole of the morning mainly calm, barely going 2 knots with rather a strong sea. In the evening about 5 o'clock the wind got to the ENE & increased to a fine breeze. The ship going about 4 & 5 knots & from midnight to 4 o'clock the wind blew so fresh she increased her rate to 6 knots. This fine breeze continued all night.
