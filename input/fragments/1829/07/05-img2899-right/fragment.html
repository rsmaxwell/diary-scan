... long there as it threatened another storm, we therefor got into our boat, but had not been long on board before the rain came down in torrents with most awful thunder & lightning. It however cleared up again before we reached our landing place. These gardens are about two miles or more from Petersburg & where the people flocked to in thousands on a Sunday. The walks upon this & neighbouring islands are beautiful. We fortunately had a boat with an awning over it.

On our return took a glass of grog & went to bed
