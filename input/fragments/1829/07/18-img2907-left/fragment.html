Another lighter came down last night with the remainder of our tallow.

After breakfast, Bob, JG & I went on shore to order some boots etc. Met with Mr Bartholomew. Went with him & had our dinner on board the David. He left by the steam boat for Petersburg at 5 o'clock. Bab & I went with Stephenson to the Butchers to see the beef salted & afterwards took a stroll.

Got in 120 casks of tallow this day.
