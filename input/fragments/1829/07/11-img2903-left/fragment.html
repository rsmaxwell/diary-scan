A fine morning, but windy & dusty. This being the [day] of St Peter & St Paul, is a general holiday here & services in all the churches. Mr Lindsey his friend Mr Miggete, Bob & I walked out at 12 o'clock for a stroll. Looked into the Catholic Church where they were just concluding the service, their singing seemed to be good. Returned to my lodgings about 3 o'clock, leaving Bob to prolong his walk & had a snooze being tired. Dined at the German Club at ½ past 5 o'clock. In the evening Bob & I ...
