A continuance of frost & snow & a gale of wind from SE to SSE. Went to Newcastle to endeavour to get something for the Hope, but did not succeed. People not willing to do anything until after the meeting of coal owners. Dined with I Tinley, a party, Captain Morins & 2 Lieutenants of the childers, C Milners & sister Miss Atlen Lieutenants 80th Regiment
