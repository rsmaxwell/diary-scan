A fine day & cold. Wind ENE & E by N. Attended a general meeting of the ship owners & entered into resolutions relative to the rate of timber freights for the ensuring years & agreed not to take less than 45/- for Quebec, other ports in proportion. The meeting from the conflicting interests could come to no resolution with regard to the coal trade. Dined at home in the evening. Took wine & suppered with A Harry, A French & A Nicolson at the Albion. Called at the Mulstream.
