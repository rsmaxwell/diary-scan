Been blowing fresh all night & still continues at SE with small rain. The ship remaining down river with a strong ebb tide at the rate of about 10 knots, having got underway at day light between 5 & 6 o'clock. At 10 o'clock am abreast of Aldeburgh. Clearer weather. Top mast & lower studding sails set. Passed through the Broads about 1 o'clock with a fine breeze. At about 8 o'clock pm abreast of Happisburgh. Took our pipe & nip & turned in at 9 o'clock. A beautiful star light night. Wind about W by S
