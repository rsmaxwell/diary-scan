Turned out at ½ past 7 o'clock ship abreast of Darlington, having had a fine run all night. Wind W by S & W SW & beautiful weather at noon got round Flamborough Head close in shore. A delightful sail. Passed Scarborough about 2 o'clock & at 3 o'clock where off the south cheek of Robin Hoods Bay, had a small shower of rain, but soon cleared up & we had fine weather again & being close in shore enjoyed a most beautiful view of the coast & country. Passed Huntcliff Foot before dusk with a continuance of the breeze at about W. At 11 o'clock brought up off Tynemouth Bar, being past high water, we could only attempt to go in.
