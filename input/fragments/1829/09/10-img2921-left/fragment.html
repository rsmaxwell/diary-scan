Showery weather. Wind S W. Took a cab into the city and afterwards walked to the docks. Found the people employed hauling the ship out, intending to take her down to Dukes shore. Received from Stephenson £89.7.6 for the lathwood. With Maryann. Attended chambers. H Harry never made his appearance. Did nothing. Went by a Paddington stage to Nottingham Place where Bob Marsh & I dined with William & enjoyed ourselves much.
