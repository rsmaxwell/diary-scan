A cloudy morning. Wind WSW. Went into the city upon a Paddington stage coach rather early, to pack a hamper of special madeira up for Mr Chapman of Elseineur. J Galbraith & I thence walked down to Dowson's dock to the Hope, where we dined. The people busy caulking & repairing some soft places where she has been leaking. Left the ship between 4 & 5 o'clock.
