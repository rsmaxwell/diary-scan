A very wet day & been raining heavy all night. Wind NE. Took a Paddington dray to the city. Called at Chapmans & agreed to take 500 staves and not more unless the master wished. The agent giving me a letter pledging himself to that effect. On the Coal Exchange nothing particular there. Did not find a ship to take me to Shields. Took a coach & carried all J G's cloths down to the ship. Found her just hauled out of dock. Had some beef.
