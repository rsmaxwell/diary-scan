Been blowing a gale of wind from the NNE all night & continued with rain all day. Amused ourselves during the morning strolling about Purfleet & playing at billiards. Got our ballast on board & hauled from the wharf about 5 o'clock. Weighed anchor & sailed down to the Tower. Hope when we brought up for the night. Blowing a gale of wind. Finished a couple of glasses of whiskey punch, smoked a pipe & turned in at 9 o'clock
