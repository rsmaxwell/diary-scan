A fine day. Settled with Mr Price for the sale of the furniture & after paying his charges & received from him a bill on Mr Conolly for £18.17.6 at 2 Nov, payable at no 1 Upper Orsmond Quay Dublin & £13.5.8 cash for balance. Dined at the Careys. Met Captain DeLacy & Mr Nugent of the 30th & went to a pleasant dinner at E Butlins in the evening & kept up until after 2 o'clock in the morning
