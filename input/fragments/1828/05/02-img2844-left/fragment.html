A fine spring day with a shower of rain in the morning. Wind SW. Mr Vernon & I walked out to_____ & dined with J Fitzmaurice. The house stands about halfway up the mountain & commands a most extensive & beautiful view of the surrounding country for many miles, bounded by the Wiklow & other mountains, taking in Carlow in north 10 miles or more off & other towns. We spent a very pleasant day. Received from & wrote a letter to William
