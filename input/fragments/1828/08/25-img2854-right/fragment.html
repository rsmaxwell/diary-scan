A hot day, wind west. Went into the city by a Paddington stage. On the market, coal lower. Walked out of the city. Dined at 6 o'clock with Mrs Whenbury in Gower Street; a quiet party only Richmond there. He & I went in the evening to Vauxhall Gardens to meet Bob Marsh, Vincent & William. A great many people there, but of the inferior classes. Got home between 1 & 2 o'clock.
