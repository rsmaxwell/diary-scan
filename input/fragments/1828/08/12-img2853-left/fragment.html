Looked out a little after 4 o'clock am. Found we had got a change of wind about 2 o'clock from about WNW. An agreeable change for us, making all sail. The ship passed the floating light at 3 o'clock, reached Cromer at ____. Passed through Yarmouth Roads at 11 o'clock & brought up being high in the water, qt ½ past. Off Golaton, the Delayenfile & nancy. in company. Wind about W & by S. The latter ship from Archangel Captain Stormont came on board to dinner. At 5 pm being low-water got underway with a light breeze from the W by S & sailed out of St Nicholas Gateway & beat to the southern water highwater between 12 & 1 o'clock, when we brought up for the tide between the reefs points about 4 miles to the south of Southwold, between 1 & 2 o'clock am. After supper, turned in for the night.
