Turned out at 3 o'clock. A dismal & wet morning, blowing hard from the east. Had not light enough to get us underway until about 4 o'clock, when the whole fleet were in motion, fortunately, at last with a fair wind. Being excessively wet & unpleasant I got into bed again & lay till ½ past 10 o'clock & then found the ship off Leigh above Southend & still raining hard.
