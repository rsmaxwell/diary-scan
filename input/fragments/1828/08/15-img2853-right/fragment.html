A fine morning. Wind WNW Took the Greenwich passage boat at the tower & went down to the Hope off Greenwich & found her just getting underway for the pool, on my return pesance conveyances much surprised at meeting Watt. Carr's Walls such a bad coal than none of the Hope was sold today. Wrote to Mrs Scott. Dined with Richmond & met Tom Griffeth. Played a few rubbers in the evening & lost £3.15/- Got home to Nottingham Place at 2 o'clock am Saturday
