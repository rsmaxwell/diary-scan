A glorious day. Such a change. Quite spring after the stormy weather we had. Wind WSW Did not go into the city. At 12 o'clock walked down to Hartley the dentists in mags Building & had my teeth examined & cleaned. Called at fosby's & Bob March's & afterwards walked home with William to drufs. Dined at 7 o'clock with Mr Linskill, met Mrs & Miss Payin Mr Ro Bill & spent a pleasant day. Lady Campbell came in to tea
