At 6 o'clock am off Whitby. Wind about SE by S & SSE during the day heavy squalls with rain & a little thunder & lightning. Obliged to take in sail until at last we only carried two close reefed topsails, reefed topsail & fore topmast of sail before dark the wind got to W by S & WSW when off Robin Hood's bay & increased to a heavy gale & tremendous sea which lasted all night, continued on the starboard tack until 6 o'clock Sunday morning. Still blowing a gale with heavy sea. Up all night
