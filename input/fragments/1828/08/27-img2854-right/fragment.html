Another beautiful day. Wind SE. left town at 9 o'clock am. By the Portsmouth coach with Bob Marsh, where we arrived at ½ past 5 o'clock, crossed over by the steam boat to Ryde to stay with Mr Lee. Dined between 7 & 8 o'clock. Mr Lee living at Navaino House, beautifully. Situated about ½ a mile west of the town, upon the side of the hill which descended to the sea of which it commands a most beautiful view, with the mainland, Portsmouth
