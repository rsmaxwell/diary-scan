Rather cloudy in the morning. Wind NE but afterwards turned out an exceedingly fine day. Started at ½ past 10 o'clock am with the following party in two sociable & age upon a tour round the island viz: Mr Rebecca & Laura Lee, Captain & Mrs Lewis, F Barclay, Bob Marsh, WBS, self & two servants. Stopped & saw Shanklin Chin, called & left our luggage at Ventnor. Drove on to & saw Appuldurcombe the seat of Lord Yarborough containing several fine spachlings & finally situated. Returned to Ventnor to dinner & slept. A very pleasant & agreeable party
