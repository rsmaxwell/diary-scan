Another fine day. Wind SW Went to the city yesterday an the Paddington coach engaged during the morning with Captain Dawson of the Dalrynple swearing offedaort respecting timber & walked out of the city. Called upon Bob Marsh. We talked usefe Mr Vincent hired a boat & rowed up to Putney, where we had Lea Mattow upofer & returned to town at 9 o'clock after a very pleasant evening
