Another fine day. Wind SW Went to the city as yesterday on the Paddington coach. Engaged during the morning with Captain Dawson of the Dalryaple swearing affidavit respecting timbers etc. walked out of the city. Called upon Bob Marsh. We went with Mr Vincent, hired a boat & rowed up to Putney, where we had tea, mutton chops etc. & returned to town at 9 o'clock after a very pleasant evening
