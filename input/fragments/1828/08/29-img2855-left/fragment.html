A very foggy morning, wind NE. Cleared up towards 12 o'clock & turned out a delightful day. Rebecca Captain & Mr Lewis, F Barclay, Bob Marsh, William & I went in a sailing boat to Portsmouth to see the Victory & much pleased with our trip. The wind being against us, we were obliged to land & walk about two miles to Gosport. Returned to Navarino at ½ past 7 o'clock to dinner
