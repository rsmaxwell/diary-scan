A cloudy day with heavy showers of rain. Had some conversation with Mr Walker. Determined upon going to Dublin to administer to the amount of sale of furniture only & take out letters of guardianship. Received from Mr Cleer £1.11 for whisky & malting. Dined at home Finished my letter to William & put it into the post. Ordered a car to be with me at 7 o'clock in the morning if fine to go to Athy to see Mr Pope
