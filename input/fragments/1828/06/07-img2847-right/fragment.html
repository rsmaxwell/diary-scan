The first thing in the morning called upon Mr Carmichael but busy engaged made an appointment with for 1 o'clock. Walked down to see the guard returned at the castle. Much to my surprise, met captain flu ie Buller. At 1 o'clock saw kpe & then went to Mr Stocks the Proctor who filled up a form for the children to sign that they are willing. I should administer in my ofmercor very inconsistent. Sent it off by post to Mr Vernon. Dined at Meckens
