A fine day with a few light showers of rain. Mr Walker received the Lord Chancellors orders found Mr Carmichael & servers Mrs Falkener with a copy & notice. Entered into an agreement with Mr Digan to grant him leave when legally authorized of the house in Dublin street & gave him profession upon his paying one years rent in advance reg:£35 less £2 allowed him for the difference in time between the 30th May the day the lease is to be taken from to this day. Dined with Dr Reed
