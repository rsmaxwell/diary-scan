Again at Newcastle. Saw J Smith Senior & Junior: the old gent: very kind, asked me to dine, "no go" entered upon the subject of the Ariel, showed them W S Brewer's accounts, they seemed to be of our opinion & against any arbitration. Dined with Dixon, had a long conversation about his assisting me. He was extremely kind in his expressions, but said it was out of his power, from his heavy advances to others & having heavy payments to make himself to but that he would consider about it. memo: Its what I expected, not much disappointed. A promise ought to be held sacred. Cold day with rain afternoon
