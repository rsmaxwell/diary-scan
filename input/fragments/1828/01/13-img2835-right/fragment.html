After a blowy and rainy night, on rising this morning, found the snow nearly dissolved. Wind E to ESE & very heavy sea upon the bar, a few ships arrived. Another disappointment! Received a letter from Sir D Smith stating the Dukes inability to comply with my request for a loan. Every person poor when you want assistance! Adversities the time for trying friendship, in prosperity you have professions enough. I've oft been told, but I have now ascertained it's a d-d selfish world we live in; every one for himself. ‘tis almost a crime to be liberal & kind hearted
