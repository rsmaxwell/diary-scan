A beautiful morning & sharp frost. Wind SSW. Walked into Newcastle. Took the Hope on for a turn of townley & gave notice to the Hope Club of my intention to lay her up. Received from Carr & Co £21.6/- on account of WBS. Balance of freight acct o/s Hope. Came down to Shields to dinner & in the evening attended the whist club. The Hope got under the crane to take her manure out for Mr Junior Finewick of Preston Villa. Gave Mrs Scott a draft on Chapmans for £75 her annuity.
