A dull morning. Wind N by S towards evening the wind got up & increased to a perfect gale from the S by W & excessively cold. Understand that several ships, all on shore, some near Sunderland. Walked with Mr Mark & called upon Captain Bunbury & Mr Hopkins. Dined at home. In the evening went to the Commercial to see Mr Ildeston who was waiting there for Mr Tibbs. Received an invitation & suppered with the committee of Association for prosecuting felons. Sat rather late.
