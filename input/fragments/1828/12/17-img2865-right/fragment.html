A little rain in the morning. Wind W & SW. A pleasant afternoon. Dined on board the Combatant, Captain Barnes. A ship of the Tinleys, an agreeable party consisting of J Tinley, A Harry, Mr Boswell, Captain Webb, W Barnes, Mr Richardson & self, went to the play at ½ price to pass an hour but returned home at 10 o'clock at which hour Mrs Scott had made up her mind I should be in. Her conduct towards me so pointed & bad that I have determined to go to lodgings as I neither have nor do I wish to give her any annoyance at the same time. I don't choose to submit to it myself, for it seems her temper & disposition neither to be happy & comfortable herself or to let others be so.
