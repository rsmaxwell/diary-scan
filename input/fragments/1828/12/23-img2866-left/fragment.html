Rather a damp day. Wind E & ESE. Dined at I Tinleys. A pleasant day. There Captain Bumbury, Mr Hopkins 80th Regiment, Mr Parr & Doctor White. Afterwards went to the ball. Very well attended for the first time. Upwards of 60 there. Danced with a Miss Paine of Faversham, a very fine girl.

Paid Reed Appccenton one quarters wages, £1.8.9 Repaid Exors £50 borrowed of them. Gave Stephenson £25 to pay wages
