A cold day with showers of rain & some hail. Wind N & NNE a few ships got to sea at considerable risk with steam boats although it was high. The Hilton Lolifte & United Kingdom Steam Boats arrived after a long and stormy & long passage from London, the latter put in for a supply of coal & sailed again. Dined with I Tindley & in the evening walked to Preston & took tea with Miss Kilse
