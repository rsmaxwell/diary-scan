It began very early in the morning to rain & blow hard & it increased to a heavy gale. Nearly all on board sick. Obliged about 12 o'clock to lay to not being able to see land or make the floating light. The weather so thick. Between 1 & 2 o'clock made the light & got into Liverpool at 3 o'clock after a very stormy passage across of20 houses Landed very wet. Got Emma to bed, much exhausted from the fatigues & sickness We stopped at the Angel Bise
