A fine morning. Rather cloudy. Wind N. Wrote to William. J Harry & Mr Jamison called upon Mr I Called upon & saw poor old Miss Falkman who seemed much affected & grieved at dear Bopy's death. Took a walk to Clogrennane, coll Rockforts seat with Mr Vernon & the boys. Caught in heavy shower of rain on our way home got wet through. The boys dined with us
