Blowing hard all night with rain towards morning. Got up at 8 o'clock am. Wind just changed to WNW, rather squally. Ship off Cromer. At 11 o'clock abreast of the floating light. Blowing hard from West. Ship under foresail & reefed topsail, mainsail & double reefed main topsail & reefed trysail. At 2 o'clock pm double reefed fore-top-sail. At 3 o'clock made the Spurn [Point] being off the floating light. Still blowing strong from the West
