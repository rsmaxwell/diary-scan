Beautiful weather. Wind SW & WSW. Mr Culley left us this morning. Out shooting from 12 o'clock to 3 o'clock towards Amble & killed 2 ½ braces. Shot very well today. Tom & Fanny drove this morning to Brinkburn. Fanny remained there & Tom returned in the evening. Sent a brace of birds to Mrs Scott and a brace to J Tinley
