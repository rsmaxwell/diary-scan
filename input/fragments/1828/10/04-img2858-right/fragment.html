A very wet morning. Wind WSW. Cleared up towards the middle of the day & turned out a fine afternoon. Went down to the ship about 1 o'clock pm. Found her nearly out. Watt came on board. Sailed about 3 o'clock & got below Woolwich & brought up, where Watt left us. A breeze springing up got underway again about 9 o'clock against the flood but obliged again to let go our anchor about 11 o'clock for want of wind! For the night William slept on board. Brought today a life preserver for 18/- Some white & coloured neckcloths for £2
