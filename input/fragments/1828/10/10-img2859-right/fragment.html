A fine day. Wind WSW with a shower of rain at 1 o'clock. Mr Mark went on board the Hope with me & had some soup at 4 o'clock pm. The Hope load up & at 5 o'clock got to sea with a fine leading wind & beautiful weather & prospect of making a quick passage. Burdon Main loaded her for 15 keels & 6 char. Dined with J Tinley, J Dale, mark & Temple there. Won at whist 16/6 Gave Stephenson 35 to pay pilotage etc. with.
