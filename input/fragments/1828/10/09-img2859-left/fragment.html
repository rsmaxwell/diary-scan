A very fine morning. Wind NW. Made the Hope's book up. On board the ship under the spout. Got Swin Kuls in. Set the shew for-topmast backstays. The Lady Jane arrived from Greenland with 15 fish, a full ship. Gave Stephenson £30. Made an agreement with the washerwoman for the quarter at 1 ½ guinea. Dined with I Tindley. Won at whist 4/-
