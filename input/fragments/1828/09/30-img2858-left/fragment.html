A dull morning during the day showers of rain. Wind SW. Walked into the city. Brought a new hat of olephant. Paid £3 for the Hopes ballast & took a Greenwich boat into the pool to her. Gave the mate charge for the passage to Shields. Returned to the city & had a basic of Larthe soup at Bleadens. Afterwards took a Paddington Stage & went with William to see the Zoological Society garden in the Regent park. Well worth the walk. In the evening went to the Argyle rooms & was much amused with Fumtas chin
