A beautiful day. Wind NE. Changed to NW & N with thunder & lightning & apparently heavy rain in Hampshire, but returned to the old quarter at night. F Barclay left us this morning for London. Sorry to loose him, a pleasant fellow. Mr Lee & Laura in the chaise & the rest walked to Binstead to see the abbey of which there is little remaining, after lunching on the grass with difficulty & getting our feet covered with mud. We returned home by the beach.
