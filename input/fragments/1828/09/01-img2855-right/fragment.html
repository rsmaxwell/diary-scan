A fine morning. Wind still from the NE. F Barclay, WBS & I bathed & after breakfast started for Newport where after passing through a pretty country, we arrived about 2 o'clock, stopping to see Carisbrooke Castle on the way. After lunch continued our journey to East Cows, where we dined. For Previously crossing the water to see West Cowes & in the evening, after a most delightful tour & favored with most beautiful weather. We all returned safe to Navarino House much pleased & satisfied. Expenses of the tour £3.10.
