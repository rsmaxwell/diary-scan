A good deal of rain in the night. Wind SW. Left Ryde at 9 o'clock am. Steam for Portsmouth. Took an outside place in the Rocket coach & arrived in London at ½ past 7 o'clock pm having had a few showers of rain on the road. Got a bed in Nottingham Place. William out of town at his friend Oliphants. Found a note from him that Stephenson had been obliged to leave the Hope & return to the North in consequence of the illness of his uncle. Spent a very pleasant month with my friends, the Lees at Ryde& sorry to leave there.
