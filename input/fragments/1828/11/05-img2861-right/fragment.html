Got up at ½ past 6 o'clock am. A fine morning. Wind at SE. Breakfasted & rode to Morepeth with Tom Smith, market & fair day there. Left Morpeth by the Shields coach & got home at 4 o'clock. To dinner. Mrs Scott very well. The Hope arrived this afternoon & fixed for Wellington to be loaded on Tuesday. Received a letter from William, the sale with balance now for £73.18/-
