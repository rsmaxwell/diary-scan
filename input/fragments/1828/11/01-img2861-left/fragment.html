Fine weather. Wind NW. Out shooting, no sport, the dog being tired by having been at Warkworth & the birds wild. Dined at Mr A Moises. Amble & spent a very pleasant day. The party consisting of Major Mr & Miss Clatterburn, Mr & Mrs Moises, Mr Elliott, Tom & Fanny Smith, self & Mr I Ord. Received a letter from master of the Hope being to sail on Thursday from London
