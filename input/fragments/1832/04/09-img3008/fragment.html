A dull cool morning. Wind ESE. Got up at 4 o'clock & went by the coach from South Shields to Stockton, got there at ½ past 9 o'clock. Walked to Middlesbrough & saw Mr Harris about fixing the Hope. He is to send me a charter. Returned by the 2 o'clock coach & got to North Shields at 8 o'clock. With Bess
