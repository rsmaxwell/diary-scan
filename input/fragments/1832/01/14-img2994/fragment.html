A fine morning. A slight frost. Wind NNW. Went to N Castle by Steam. Called & saw Mr Armstrong for WBS & wrote to the latter not to sell the Hope. Saw Angers about the carriage, who can neither buy or sell. Returned by steam. Dined with Dr Drury. At the Billiard room. Attended the Whist Club at Tibbs. A fine moonlit night. With Bess
