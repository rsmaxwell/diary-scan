Note

The following note appears at the front of the diary which appears to be a bet on a result in the 1832 General election

2/1 This Mr Bell is returned for the southern Division of the county the next election

A Richardson, Bank

Mr Webster bets him the same

 

5/5 This Mr Bell is returned for the southern Division of the county

A C Wright

 

Nov 1832

Mr Richardson has has half his bet
