A cold damp morning. Wind ESE. A good deal of rain & sleet. Wrote to WBS & remitted him two promise notes £142.15.6 & £71.2.2 to take up the Hopes cargo bill of £211.12/-. Gave a draft on Chapmans for the amount of the bills. Dined at home. At the billiard room in the afternoon & suppered with Lockwood
