Northumbrian Regimental dinner at the Turf at 6 o'clock. A fine morning. Been a shower of rain. Wind WNW. Went to Newcastle by steam. Dined at 6 o'clock. 23 officers at dinner & strangers. Mr Beerrall of Broom Park, D Dixon, I Cockson, Pollard, Lambert, Reed & Small. Spent a pleasant day & left the party before 1 o'clock in the morning. Some remained till 5 o'clock
