A beautiful morning. Mr & Mrs Ilderton drove us over to Ilderton. They stopped to attend Mr Howey's sale. Robert & I went on in the carriage to Benton beyond Wooler. I walked from there to West Newton & fished down the Glen, beautiful stream but had poor sport, being a bright day & water fine. Suppered at Benton & went by coach at ½ past 11 o'clock to the GameKeepers Cottage at Ildeston to sleep. Excessively fatigued
