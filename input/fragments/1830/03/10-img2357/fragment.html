Dull & thick weather & rather cold. Blowing fresh at NSW. Mr & Mrs Dixon & I Smith came down today for the purpose of having Mrs Scott's will read & which not a little surprised all of us, a more extraordinary one perhaps never was: By it I am left the very handsome legacy of £10 !!! the same to my brother, plainly proving & confirming what her feelings towards us throughout life must have been, inveterately bad, & carried to the grave with her. Peace go with her. I forgive her For all the mischief & misery she Has caused in our family. The party left us in the afternoon.
