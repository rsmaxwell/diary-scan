Fine weather blowing fresh at WNW. Received a letter from WBS of his arrival in London & also one from I Smith to say he will dine & stay all night with me on the 1st May. Wrote to WBS. Dined at home. Saw Mr & Mrs Ildegton, he very lame with the gout. Spent the evening at Allens, got home at ½ past 2 o'clock
