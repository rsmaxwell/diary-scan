Fine weather again & glafs risen considerably since yesterday evening about 1 ½ inch & up to fair. Wind NNW. Attended morning wives. Dined with I Tealeg, Mrs Cumliff & keifs of the These. Walked to Tynemouth in the afternoon; At the S Cellegator off several ships went to sea, bound foreign, the Broderweck, 3 Brothers, Dalsymple, Hannah, Equity les. Spent the evening at Tealegs A heavy rain at night.
