Mild weather & blowing fresh from the WNW. Saw Brown about his continuancy in the George. He is to give us an address by Thursday Took the steam boat & went to Newcastle Could not get anything for the Hope Lunched with A Weyhtos & at 3 o'clock called in Westgate Street. I found Mrs Dixon just arrived from Brenbuch & Joys Cow Dined & stayed all night with them
