A fine morning. Wind changed about during the day. Dined early, the Decghtow called & dined with us & in the afternoon Harry & I walked to the races, a great many people there being so beautiful a day. The rest of the party went to hear the choral society at the church. In the evening A H & I went to the Ball . Very few people there, Mr Craig behind very ill.
