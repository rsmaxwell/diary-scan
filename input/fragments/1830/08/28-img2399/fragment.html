A wet morning having been a deluge of rain in the night & blowing hard from NE. Wind changed to NW & nearly calm. After breakfast turned out a very fair day. Emma came from school. WBS went to Newcastle to see D Dixon. Moor & his brother called, after a great deal of conversation, agreed to let him continue on the farm should he exert himself to pay off the arrears. His brother promised his abstinence & to continue his security. Emma & I walked round by the wells to Tynemouth. Dined at home at 5 o'clock. At the Duke in the evening.
