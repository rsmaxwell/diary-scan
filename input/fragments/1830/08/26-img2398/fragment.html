A cloudy morning. Been rain in the night. Wind N to NE & WNW. Turned out a very fine day. Received a letter from J Chapman. William and I went out with our guns & walked to Marsden Rock by the sands. Got to the top of the rock, a man having made steps up. Got a lunch there & on our way home took a few glasses of punch with the sea breeze & Phasine Society who has this tent pitched on the cliffs.
