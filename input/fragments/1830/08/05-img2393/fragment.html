A pleasant morning wind NW. Walked to Tynemouth with Allen & balled wine at houses. Remained in the town all the afternoon expecting Williams's arrival in the steam boat from London. Walked to Tynemouth in the evening. The boat arrived off the bar at ½ past 9 o'clock but being thick could not see her. The Shields Pafseeyer landed in the Haven & WBS got home about 11 o'clock. Received the rents & paid £70 cats chapmans on account of the Exors
