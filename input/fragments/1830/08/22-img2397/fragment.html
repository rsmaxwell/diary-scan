A beautiful day. Wind NE. Went to church in the morning. Mr Mark preached. Received a letter from Stephenson dated Archangel the 15th July of his boxing load & that he had brought 57 barrels of tar on ship account, but the merchant refusing to give him deck freight. The amount of the bill drawn £120.1.1. Wrote to J Chapman to sell the tar.

Walked to Tynemouth. A great many people there. Dined with J Huntly at 5 o'clock & walked again to Tynemouth in the evening when it unfortunately began to rain & dispersed the people.
