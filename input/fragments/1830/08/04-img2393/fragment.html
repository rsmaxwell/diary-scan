A fine morning. Wind variable during the day W & SSE with rain in the afternoon. After hard fighting between the Parisians & the military where about 8 or 10000 were killed, the former drove the military out of the city & established pro: term: a provisional government & appointing the Duke of Orleans retired general of the kingdom which he accepted. Charles the X & his minions flown. The Lord Khoas where the French with much credit to themselves have effected the revolution without those outrages which characterized the former. Dined with Doctor Drourg. He & Richardson afterwards came & took wine with me.
