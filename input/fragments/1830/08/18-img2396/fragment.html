A cloudy morning. Wind NNE. Paid £16.10.3 from the money for Hope. At 12 o'clock started from the church with a large party. Picnic for Seaton Dilaual where ww dined in the garden, afterwards danced on the lawn having the band of the 82nd Regiment & spent a more delightful day. Drank tea at Holywell & got home before 9 o'clock
