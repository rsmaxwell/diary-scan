A beautiful morning Wind little at SE. Got up early & walked round by Tynemouth & Cullercoats, called upon Mr Hewetson & returned to breakfast. Received a letter from WBS with the Hopes coal freight account enclosing Metcalfrs & Drury's note for £79.18.2 Which I paid into chapmans. Took another long walk with H Harry & Peart round by the head of the town, Burdon Main, height Charton, Belly Hill & Preston & got home to dinner. Spent the latter part of the evening at Alderneys.
