A cold morning. Wind NW Saw the Northhumberland fox hounds from Benton show off at Gosforth. Dixon & I walked upto Newcastle. Saw Tom Smith, walked to see the horses being fair team. Sold an old silver spoon cracked & gave five shillings in addition for a new one for Emma. Took the steam boat at 2 o'clock. Came home to dinner. Wrote to WBS expecting Miss Gauntley & Mary Carfs. Wrote unto C Richmond that I could not meet him at Dersham on Saturday. Albion
