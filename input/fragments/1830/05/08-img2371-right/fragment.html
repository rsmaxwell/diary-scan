A dull think morning with wind SE blowing fresh. Dined with I Finely, met char Nicholas & in the evening went to the whist club at I Newghts. A very stormy night, a gale of wind at ESE with torrents of rain. Gabe Stephenson a debt on Chapmans for £30 & got £5 for self. Gave G Stafs £1 on account of wages
