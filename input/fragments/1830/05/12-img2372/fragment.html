Fairer and warmer weather. Robert Ilderstow & I had an early breakfast & rode over the river till below Hedghey, where we fished until it began to rain when we took shelter at a mill & then walked up to Rosea an estate of the Ildestonn's. Had tea & eggs & rode home in the evening, when we found Mrs Crosby & Mrs Strothard had arrived from Newcastle on a visit. Had better sport today, brought home a few good trout
