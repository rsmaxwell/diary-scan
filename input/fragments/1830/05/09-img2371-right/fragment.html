All last night marsh dismal & stormy blowing a gale of wind at ESE with a deluge of rain & still continuous Did not go to church. Employed myself in writing to WBS & G Farell. Gave Stephenson a draft upon Chapmans exors at £14.11.7 ( £5 cook + £9.11.7 Balance of after) Dined with a Harry. Called on Webb in the evening who had gout a which prevents him going to Mr Aldertons At the Duke of York in evening. Weather inadesater
