A continuance of fresh weather & been blowing very hard all night from the SE. One ship has got upon the Heard Sand, another upon the rocks & two riding off the Bar. One having cut away her masts & the other with main mast gone. Both these ships & the one on the Heard Sand got into the harbour. Walked to Tynemouth with Nicholson & Cunningham. Dined at home. In the evening H harry, Nicholson & I went to the Duke of York where we found Mr Heweson
