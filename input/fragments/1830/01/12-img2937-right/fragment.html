Last night very stormy. A gale from the NE with heavy showers of sleet & hail.

Received a letter in the name of the Bank, but evidently written by that little puppy F Chapman without consulting his partners, desiring me to pay the balance of my account with them of some £106. Called & saw Spence who seemed ignorant of such a letter having been written. Told him what I thought of such conduct. I having ample security in their hands.

Dined at home. At the Duke of York with Mr Hewson, Nicholson, Cunningham & Wheatley. Received of newsroom £3.10/- for WBD
