Another fine morning & a very intense frost. Wind about west. Walked with H Nicholson to the Ridges at High Cherton. Had a little scaling, soon tired. Walked round by Tynemouth. Returned to dinner at 3 o'clock. In the evening walked again to Tynemouth & took wine, played a rubber & suppered with Mr Brown & a party at the Star & Garter. Not my favourite. Got home at 1 o'clock. Frost again & a very wet night.
