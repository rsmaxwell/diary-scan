A very unpleasant raw damp morning. Wind SSE

A serious accident has befallen Mrs Scott. This morning having fallen off the bed & broken her thigh. It is only a simple fracture & having got it set immediately, she is suffering as little pain as could possibly be expected. Wrote to Mrs Dixon to come down immediately. Sent a messenger off to Togston. Dined at J Tinleys. Met Mr Mark there. At the Mulstrum
