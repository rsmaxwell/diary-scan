A showery morning Wind NE. Been a great deal of rain in the night. Sea high. Paid Gibson butcher bill ¼ of year £4.9/- also executors of Scott painer £3.8- for the home & £12.19/- property. Walked to Tynemouth & called upon two officers of the 15th regiment Hind & [ ] afterwards. Walked up & dined at the Point with Sadby. Attended the whist club in the evening & the Mulstrum
