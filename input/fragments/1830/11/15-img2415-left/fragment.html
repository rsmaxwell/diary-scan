A fine cold morning Wind WSW. Received a letter from WBS with Hope's archangel account but not reliance the delivered very little more than good deals Paid Robert & Leadbetter their ¼ year wages £10.11/- draft on chapmans exor account Dined at home. Called on J Young Paid Mrs Wright for Emma Galbraiths education £13.3.9 at the theatre & Mulstrum in the evening
