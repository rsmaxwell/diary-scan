The thaw continues & the snow has disappeared. Blowing a gale of wind at WNW. Went by Dunn's coach to Newcastle Fixed the Hope for the Bursaton Walls to be loaded on Monday. Gave Stephenson orders to lay her the shore line. Dined at home at 3 then took wine with I Craig & afterwards spent the evening At Tynemouth Castle with Captain French of The 80th. A large Hulk sailed today. Ann Maxwell delivered of a fine boy
