A fine morning Rather frosty wind fresh at NW. Got up at 7 o'clock and went on board the Hope. The pilot on board but decided to not venture to take her out, it being in bad tide & threw feet sea upon the bar. Received a letter from WBS & one from Tom Lee. Answered Tom Lees. Dined at home & spent the afternoon at the billiard table. In the evening, heavy rain & wind to NNE. At the Duke of York with H Harry & Nicholson
