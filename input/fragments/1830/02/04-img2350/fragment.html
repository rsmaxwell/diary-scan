A fine morning (wind NW), Frosty with a show of showers. After Breakfast Dixon & I had the beagles out & started above base which We left them running & walked Into Newcastle & not being able to get anything for the Hope, I got into a gig & came down to Shields Dined with the whist club at the Commercial, spent a pleasant day In the evening at the Card club and Afterwards at the Mulstrum
