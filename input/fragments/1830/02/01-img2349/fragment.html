A cold winters day, Wind fresh at about East with Showers of snow. Attended the sale Of the Glattons stores upon the Low Lights Quay. Brought a Capstan for £3.1. and 23 faths of 1 3/16 mock chain quite new at 17/6 a cwt. Played a few pools at billiards W Peast H Nickolson and all at the Duke of York in the evening.
