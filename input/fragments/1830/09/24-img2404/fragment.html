A fine morning with a fresh breeze at WSW. The Hope got to sea about ½ past 6 o'clock. At 7 o'clock turned out & walked to Tynemouth. Got £5 from Chapmans. Paid Pelors £1.15. & E Reed ¼ wages £1.8.6. Walked to Tynemouth to the sale of the Suffolk's stores. Dined at home. In the afternoon A Harry & I walked to the new Quay & afterwards called at the Pig & Whistle. Spent the evening at William Harrisons
