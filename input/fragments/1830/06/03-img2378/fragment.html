A dull morning with rain. Wind SSE About 11o'clock the weather cleared up, Harry, Nicholson & I walked with I Richardson in his cable up to Mr Sadlers at the Point & then to Jarrow Slake where we had two hours fishing, but very poor sport, we afterwards landed & dined with Sadler & spent a very pleasant day. It turned out a rainy evening & we had a wet & unpleasant walk home
