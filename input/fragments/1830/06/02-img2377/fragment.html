A most beautiful day, particularly the afternoon. Wind west, Took a walk with Captain Webb to Tynemouth. Met Smiths, Hannah, I Weston. Webb took her & the Blenkensops on board the Merserard Tatter, laying too off the Castle & gave them a few hours sail to the Sunderland. Dined at home & the afternoon being so fine tempted H Harry, for the sake of the walk to go to the tents on the sands but there was no racing. One match
