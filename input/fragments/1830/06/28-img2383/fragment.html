A dull cloudy morning with appearance of rain. Wind SE. Received an account of the poor vicar's death at ¼ past 8 o'clock on Saturday morning to the inexpressible sorrow of the a alecow. All the ships be hoisted their colours half mast high. Walked to Tynemouth. Dined at home. In the afternoon a heavy thunder storm with showers at intervals. Went to see A Harry in the evening who has got a light attack of gout.
