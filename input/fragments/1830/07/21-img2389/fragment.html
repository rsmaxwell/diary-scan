Wind NNW & NW. A most beautiful Day. Breakfasted at the George with Rec Hodgson who afterwards with his friends set off for Blyth where they were to decide with the Premium. At ½ past 1 o'clock, Harry, Drury & I took a boat & rowed up to Sadless to dinner. I Dale also there. We spent a very pleasant day it being his birthday. Took a glass of wine & water with Dale on our return. Received a letter from Fanny Soneth informing us of Emma being rather unwell & requesting if I would allow to stay with them a little longer, which I agreed to.
