After a night of heavy rain the weather cleared up a little. Wind SE & thick. Received a letter from Ryan L Parkinson, Sackville Street, Dublin stating that nothing had been done with the Fitznanances affairs. At 12 o'clock this solders with the band marched out the garrison & afterwards with the peaceful inhabited in procession & winner racecourse of people marched round the town & proclaimed king William 4th. In the afternoon a party of us dined at the George Tavern & spent a very pleasant day
