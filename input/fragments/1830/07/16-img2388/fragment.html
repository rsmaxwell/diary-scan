A pleasant morning But again blowing hard at WSW It large concussion of people with A professional gentleman with Baudr, flags to went to Chester Whence they met the Bale & brought him through the upper part of town to the George Tavern after which low sat down to an excellent & well arranged discussion nearly 200 freehold all went off well. The latter part of the evening a glorious scene of drunk cuffs and confusion amongst the lower set of freeholders.
