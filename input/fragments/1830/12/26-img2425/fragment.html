A continuance of frosty weather. A good deal of snow fallen in the night. Wind WNW. Did not go to church. Wrote to B B Galbraith. Received an account these H Harry, A Clark & Berges of the sale of 84 more than Hope coals at 35/-. Dined with J Tealey. The officers of HMS Samarang & Childers there & Goshing RA
