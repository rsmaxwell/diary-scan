A fine morning & hard frost with a little snow on the ground. Wind NW. On board the Hope & the Lightning Government Steamer came down here with 150 marines for the garrison at Tynemouth. A beautiful copal of elegantly fit up.

Dined at home & in the evening attended the theatre with Hargraves beautiful & bespeake of the Bachelors of Noshules. Finished off at the Albion. Sleet and rain at night.
