In the night it began to blow very hard from the NNE. & continued pretty near a gale the whole of the day from that to NE with heavy showers of hail & snow & high sea. Another catic put in making two, live government steamers, a Green Boy, 28 gun frigate in the harbour. Attended a public meeting for the purpose of entering this resolution with regard to the Rail road. Dined at home. At the billiard & news rooms & finished at the Duke of York. Got £5 of chapman
