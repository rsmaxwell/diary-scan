During the night the wind veered round to the NE. Blowing half a gale and a heavy sea came on. The HMS Childers moored into Peggy's hole again. Cunningham dined with me. At the billiard room in the afternoon. J Agusley got his discharge today from hews & billiard room having given himself up to drunkenness. At the theatre in the evening. The bespeak of the officers of the garrison.

P J Hutchison £2.16.3 averages
