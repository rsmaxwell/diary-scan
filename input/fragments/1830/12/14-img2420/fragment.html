A very wet raw unpleasant morning. Wind W by N. The Hope got to sea in the afternoon without touching the bar, there being a great deal of sea on. Some had accounts of the ships that sailed on Saturday many struck very heavily on the bar & some in consequence the crew not being able to Kup them, foundered & all the people drowned. At least we had an account of one & another having run on shore. Dined at the Albion, a bed dance & a very good one, spent a pleasant day. In visiting Bradshaw & H Harry, chain & vince. 12 percent
